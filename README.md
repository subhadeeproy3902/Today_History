
# Today's History 📜

20th August 2024


## Year - 2020
 [Joe Biden](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Joe_Biden "Joe Biden") gives his acceptance speech virtually for the [2020 Democratic presidential nomination](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Joe_Biden_2020_presidential_campaign "Joe Biden 2020 presidential campaign") at the [2020 Democratic National Convention](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2020_Democratic_National_Convention "2020 Democratic National Convention").

## Year - 2016
 [Fifty-four people are killed](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/August_2016_Gaziantep_bombing "August 2016 Gaziantep bombing") when a suicide bomber detonates himself at a Kurdish wedding party in [Gaziantep](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaziantep "Gaziantep"), [Turkey](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey "Turkey").

## Year - 2014
 Seventy-two people are killed in Japan's [Hiroshima Prefecture](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hiroshima_Prefecture "Hiroshima Prefecture") by a series of [landslides](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2014_Hiroshima_landslides "2014 Hiroshima landslides") caused by a month's worth of rain that fell in one day.

## Year - 2012
 A [prison riot](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Yare_prison_riot "Yare prison riot") in the [Venezuelan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Venezuela "Venezuela") capital, [Caracas](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Caracas "Caracas"), kills at least 20 people.

## Year - 2008
 [Spanair Flight 5022](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanair_Flight_5022 "Spanair Flight 5022"), from [Madrid](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Madrid "Madrid"), Spain to [Gran Canaria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gran_Canaria "Gran Canaria"), skids off the runway and crashes at [Barajas Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Adolfo_Su%C3%A1rez_Madrid%E2%80%93Barajas_Airport "Adolfo Suárez Madrid-Barajas Airport"). Of the 172 people on board, 146 die immediately, and eight more later die of injuries sustained in the crash.

## Year - 2007
 [China Airlines Flight 120](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/China_Airlines_Flight_120 "China Airlines Flight 120") catches fire and explodes after landing at [Naha Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Naha_Airport "Naha Airport") in [Okinawa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Okinawa_Prefecture "Okinawa Prefecture"), Japan.

## Year - 2006
 [Sri Lankan Civil War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sri_Lankan_Civil_War "Sri Lankan Civil War"): Sri Lankan Tamil politician and former MP [S. Sivamaharajah](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/S._Sivamaharajah "S. Sivamaharajah") is shot dead at his home in [Tellippalai](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tellippalai "Tellippalai").

## Year - 2002
 A group of Iraqis opposed to the regime of [Saddam Hussein](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Saddam_Hussein "Saddam Hussein") take over the [Iraqi](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ba%27athist_Iraq "Ba'athist Iraq") [embassy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplomatic_mission "Diplomatic mission") in Berlin, Germany for five hours before releasing their hostages and surrendering.

## Year - 1998
 [U.S. embassy bombings](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1998_United_States_embassy_bombings "1998 United States embassy bombings"): The United States launches [cruise missile attacks](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Infinite_Reach "Operation Infinite Reach") against alleged [al-Qaeda](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Al-Qaeda "Al-Qaeda") [camps](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghan_training_camp "Afghan training camp") in [Afghanistan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan") and a [suspected chemical weapons plant](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Al-Shifa_pharmaceutical_factory "Al-Shifa pharmaceutical factory") in [Sudan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan "Sudan") in retaliation for the [August 7](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/August_7 "August 7") bombings of American embassies in [Kenya](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kenya "Kenya") and [Tanzania](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tanzania "Tanzania").

## Year - 1998
 The [Supreme Court of Canada](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_Canada "Supreme Court of Canada") [rules](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Reference_Re_Secession_of_Quebec "Reference Re Secession of Quebec") that [Quebec](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Quebec "Quebec") cannot legally secede from Canada without the federal government's approval.

## Year - 1997
 [Souhane massacre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Souhane_massacre "Souhane massacre") in [Algeria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Algeria "Algeria"); over 60 people are killed and 15 kidnapped.

## Year - 1995
 The [Firozabad rail disaster](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Firozabad_rail_disaster "Firozabad rail disaster") kills 358 people in [Firozabad](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Firozabad "Firozabad"), [India](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India").

## Year - 1993
 After rounds of secret negotiations in Norway, the [Oslo Accords](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Oslo_Accords "Oslo Accords") are signed, followed by a public ceremony in Washington, D.C. the following month.

## Year - 1992
 In India, [Meitei language](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Meitei_language "Meitei language") (officially known as [Manipuri language](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Manipuri_language "Manipuri language")) was included in the [scheduled languages' list](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Eighth_Schedule_to_the_Constitution_of_India "Eighth Schedule to the Constitution of India") and made one of the [official languages of the Indian Government](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Languages_with_official_status_in_India "Languages with official status in India").

## Year - 1991
 [Estonia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Estonia "Estonia"), occupied by and incorporated into the Soviet Union in 1940, issues a decision on the re-establishment of independence on the basis of [legal continuity](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/State_continuity_of_the_Baltic_states "State continuity of the Baltic states") of its pre-occupation statehood.

## Year - 1991
 [Dissolution of the Soviet Union](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dissolution_of_the_Soviet_Union "Dissolution of the Soviet Union"), [August Coup](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1991_Soviet_coup_d%27%C3%A9tat_attempt "1991 Soviet coup d'état attempt"): More than 100,000 people rally outside the Soviet Union's parliament building protesting the [coup](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Coup_d%27%C3%A9tat "Coup d'état") aiming to depose [President](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_Soviet_Union "President of the Soviet Union") [Mikhail Gorbachev](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mikhail_Gorbachev "Mikhail Gorbachev").

## Year - 1989
 The pleasure boat [<i>Marchioness</i> sinks](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Marchioness_disaster "Marchioness disaster") on the [River Thames](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/River_Thames "River Thames") following a collision. Fifty-one people are killed.

## Year - 1988
 [The Troubles](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Troubles "The Troubles"): [Eight British soldiers are killed and 28 wounded](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ballygawley_bus_bombing "Ballygawley bus bombing") when their bus is hit by an [IRA](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Provisional_Irish_Republican_Army "Provisional Irish Republican Army") roadside bomb in [Ballygawley, County Tyrone](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ballygawley,_County_Tyrone "Ballygawley, County Tyrone").

## Year - 1988
 [Iran–Iraq War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran%E2%80%93Iraq_War "Iran-Iraq War"): A ceasefire is agreed after almost eight years of war.

## Year - 1988
 "Black Saturday" of the [Yellowstone fire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Yellowstone_fires_of_1988 "Yellowstone fires of 1988") in [Yellowstone National Park](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Yellowstone_National_Park "Yellowstone National Park")

## Year - 1986
 In [Edmond, Oklahoma](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Edmond,_Oklahoma "Edmond, Oklahoma"), [U.S. Postal](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Postal_Service "United States Postal Service") employee Patrick Sherrill [guns down](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Edmond_post_office_shooting "Edmond post office shooting") 14 of his co-workers and then commits suicide.

## Year - 1977
 [Voyager program](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Voyager_program "Voyager program"): NASA launches the <i>[Voyager 2](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Voyager_2 "Voyager 2")</i> spacecraft.

## Year - 1975
 [ČSA Flight 540](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/%C4%8CSA_Flight_540 "ČSA Flight 540") crashes on approach to [Damascus International Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Damascus_International_Airport "Damascus International Airport") in [Damascus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Damascus "Damascus"), [Syria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Syria "Syria"), killing 126 people.

## Year - 1975
 [Viking program](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Viking_program "Viking program"): [NASA](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/NASA "NASA") launches the <i>[Viking 1](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Viking_1 "Viking 1")</i> planetary probe toward [Mars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mars "Mars").

## Year - 1968
 [Cold War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cold_War "Cold War"): Warsaw Pact troops [invade Czechoslovakia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Warsaw_Pact_invasion_of_Czechoslovakia "Warsaw Pact invasion of Czechoslovakia"), crushing the [Prague Spring](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prague_Spring "Prague Spring"). East German participation is limited to a few specialists due to memories of the recent war. Only Albania and Romania refuse to participate.

## Year - 1962
 The [NS <i>Savannah</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/NS_Savannah "NS Savannah"), the world's first [nuclear-powered](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nuclear_marine_propulsion "Nuclear marine propulsion") civilian ship, embarks on its maiden voyage.

## Year - 1960
 [Senegal](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Senegal "Senegal") breaks from the [Mali Federation](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mali_Federation "Mali Federation"), declaring its independence.

## Year - 1955
 [Battle of Philippeville](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Philippeville "Battle of Philippeville"): In [Morocco](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Morocco "Morocco"), a force of [Berbers](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Berbers "Berbers") from the [Atlas Mountains](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlas_Mountains "Atlas Mountains") region of [Algeria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Algeria "Algeria") raid two rural settlements and kill 77 French nationals.

## Year - 1949
 [Hungary](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary "Hungary") adopts the [Hungarian Constitution of 1949](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungarian_Constitution_of_1949 "Hungarian Constitution of 1949") and becomes a [People's Republic](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungarian_People%27s_Republic "Hungarian People's Republic").

## Year - 1948
 Soviet Consul General in [New York](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City "New York City"), [Jacob M. Lomakin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacob_M._Lomakin "Jacob M. Lomakin") is expelled by the [United States](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States"), due to the [Kasenkina Case](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kasenkina_Case "Kasenkina Case").

## Year - 1944
 World War II: The [Battle of Romania](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Romania "Battle of Romania") begins with a major [Soviet Union](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union") offensive.

## Year - 1944
 World War II: [One hundred sixty-eight captured allied airmen](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/KLB_Club "KLB Club"), including [Phil Lamason](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Phil_Lamason "Phil Lamason"), accused by the [Gestapo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gestapo "Gestapo") of being "terror fliers", arrive at [Buchenwald concentration camp](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Buchenwald_concentration_camp "Buchenwald concentration camp").

## Year - 1940
 World War II: The [Eighth Route Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Eighth_Route_Army "Eighth Route Army") launches the [Hundred Regiments Offensive](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hundred_Regiments_Offensive "Hundred Regiments Offensive"), a successful campaign to disrupt Japanese war infrastructure and logistics in occupied northern China.

## Year - 1940
 [World War II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II "World War II"): [British Prime Minister](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_the_United_Kingdom "Prime Minister of the United Kingdom") [Winston Churchill](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Winston_Churchill "Winston Churchill") makes the fourth of his famous wartime speeches, containing the line "[Never was so much owed by so many to so few](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Never_was_so_much_owed_by_so_many_to_so_few "Never was so much owed by so many to so few")".

## Year - 1940
 In Mexico City, exiled Russian revolutionary [Leon Trotsky](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Leon_Trotsky "Leon Trotsky") is fatally wounded with an ice axe by [Ramón Mercader](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ram%C3%B3n_Mercader "Ramón Mercader"). He dies the next day.

## Year - 1938
 [Lou Gehrig](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lou_Gehrig "Lou Gehrig") hits his 23rd career grand slam, a record that stood for 75 years until it was broken by [Alex Rodriguez](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alex_Rodriguez "Alex Rodriguez").

## Year - 1926
 Japan's [public broadcasting](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Public_broadcasting "Public broadcasting") company, [Nippon Hōsō Kyōkai](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/NHK "NHK") (NHK) is established.

## Year - 1920
 The [National Football League](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Football_League "National Football League") is organized as the American Professional Football Conference in [Canton, Ohio](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Canton,_Ohio "Canton, Ohio")

## Year - 1920
 The first commercial radio station, 8MK (now [WWJ](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/WWJ_(AM) "WWJ (AM)")), begins operations in [Detroit](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Detroit "Detroit").

## Year - 1914
 [World War I](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_I "World War I"): [Brussels](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Brussels "Brussels") is captured during the [German invasion of Belgium](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/German_invasion_of_Belgium_(1914) "German invasion of Belgium (1914)").

## Year - 1910
 Extreme [fire weather](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Fire_Danger_Rating_System "National Fire Danger Rating System") in the [Inland Northwest](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Inland_Northwest "Inland Northwest") of the United States causes many small wildfires to coalesce into the [Great Fire of 1910](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Fire_of_1910 "Great Fire of 1910"), burning approximately 3 million acres (12,000 km) and killing 87 people.

## Year - 1905
 [Sun Yat-sen](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sun_Yat-sen "Sun Yat-sen"), [Song Jiaoren](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Song_Jiaoren "Song Jiaoren"), and others establish the [Tongmenghui](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tongmenghui "Tongmenghui"), a [Republican](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Republicanism "Republicanism"), [anti-Qing](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Anti-Qing_sentiment "Anti-Qing sentiment") revolutionary organisation, in [Tokyo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tokyo "Tokyo"), [Japan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "Japan").

## Year - 1882
 [Tchaikovsky](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pyotr_Ilyich_Tchaikovsky "Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky")'s <i>[1812 Overture](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1812_Overture "1812 Overture")</i> debuts in Moscow, Russia.

## Year - 1866
 [President](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States "President of the United States") [Andrew Johnson](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Andrew_Johnson "Andrew Johnson") formally declares the [American Civil War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Civil_War "American Civil War") over.

## Year - 1864
 [Bakumatsu](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bakumatsu "Bakumatsu"): [Kinmon incident](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kinmon_incident "Kinmon incident"): The [Chōshū Domain](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ch%C5%8Dsh%C5%AB_Domain "Chōshū Domain") attempts to expel the [Satsuma](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Satsuma_Domain "Satsuma Domain") and [Aizu Domains](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Aizu_Domain "Aizu Domain") from Japan's imperial court.

## Year - 1858
 [Charles Darwin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Darwin "Charles Darwin") first publishes his theory of [evolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Evolution "Evolution") through [natural selection](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Natural_selection "Natural selection") in <i>The Journal of the Proceedings of the Linnean Society of London</i>, alongside [Alfred Russel Wallace](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfred_Russel_Wallace "Alfred Russel Wallace")'s same theory.

## Year - 1852
 Steamboat <i>[Atlantic](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlantic_(1848) "Atlantic (1848)")</i> sank on Lake Erie after a collision, with the loss of at least 150 lives.

## Year - 1794
 [Northwest Indian War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Northwest_Indian_War "Northwest Indian War"): United States troops force a confederacy of [Shawnee](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Shawnee "Shawnee"), [Mingo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mingo "Mingo"), [Delaware](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lenape "Lenape"), [Wyandot](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wyandot_people "Wyandot people"), [Miami](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Miami_people "Miami people"), [Ottawa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Odawa "Odawa"), [Chippewa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chippewa "Chippewa"), and [Potawatomi](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Potawatomi "Potawatomi") warriors into a disorganized retreat at the [Battle of Fallen Timbers](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Fallen_Timbers "Battle of Fallen Timbers").

## Year - 1775
 The Spanish establish the [Presidio San Augustin del Tucson](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Presidio_San_Augustin_del_Tucson "Presidio San Augustin del Tucson") in the town that became [Tucson, Arizona](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tucson,_Arizona "Tucson, Arizona").

## Year - 1710
 [War of the Spanish Succession](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_the_Spanish_Succession "War of the Spanish Succession"): A multinational army led by the Austrian commander [Guido Starhemberg](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Guido_Starhemberg "Guido Starhemberg") defeats the Spanish-Bourbon army commanded by [Alexandre Maître, Marquis de Bay](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexandre_Ma%C3%AEtre,_Marquis_de_Bay "Alexandre Maître, Marquis de Bay") in the [Battle of Saragossa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Saragossa "Battle of Saragossa").

## Year - 1707
 The first [Siege of Pensacola](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Pensacola_(1707) "Siege of Pensacola (1707)") comes to an end with the failure of the British to capture [Pensacola, Florida](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pensacola,_Florida "Pensacola, Florida").

## Year - 1672
 Former [Grand Pensionary](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grand_Pensionary "Grand Pensionary") [Johan de Witt](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Johan_de_Witt "Johan de Witt") and his brother [Cornelis](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cornelis_de_Witt "Cornelis de Witt") are lynched by a mob in [The Hague](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hague "The Hague").

## Year - 1648
 The [Battle of Lens](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Lens "Battle of Lens") is the last major military confrontation of the [Thirty Years' War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Thirty_Years%27_War "Thirty Years' War"), contributing to the signing of the [Peace of Westphalia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Peace_of_Westphalia "Peace of Westphalia") in October that year.

## Year - 1519
 Philosopher and general [Wang Yangming](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wang_Yangming "Wang Yangming") defeats [Zhu Chenhao](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhu_Chenhao "Zhu Chenhao"), ending the [Prince of Ning rebellion](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_of_Ning_rebellion "Prince of Ning rebellion") against the reign of the [Ming dynasty](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ming_dynasty "Ming dynasty")'s [Zhengde Emperor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhengde_Emperor "Zhengde Emperor").

## Year - 1467
 The [Second Battle of Olmedo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Battle_of_Olmedo "Second Battle of Olmedo") takes places as part of a succession conflict between [Henry IV of Castile](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_IV_of_Castile "Henry IV of Castile") and his half-brother [Alfonso, Prince of Asturias](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfonso,_Prince_of_Asturias_(1453%E2%80%931468) "Alfonso, Prince of Asturias (1453-1468)").

## Year - 1391
 [Konrad von Wallenrode](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Konrad_von_Wallenrode "Konrad von Wallenrode") becomes the 24th [Grand Master of the Teutonic Order](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grand_Master_of_the_Teutonic_Order "Grand Master of the Teutonic Order").

## Year - 1308
 [Pope Clement V](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Clement_V "Pope Clement V") pardons [Jacques de Molay](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacques_de_Molay "Jacques de Molay"), the last [Grand Master of the Knights Templar](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grand_Masters_of_the_Knights_Templar "Grand Masters of the Knights Templar"), absolving him of charges of [heresy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Heresy "Heresy").

## Year - 1191
 [Richard I of England](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_I_of_England "Richard I of England") initiates the [Massacre at Ayyadieh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Massacre_at_Ayyadieh "Massacre at Ayyadieh"), leaving 2,600–3,000 Muslim hostages dead.

## Year - 1083
 Canonization of the first [King of Hungary](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/King_of_Hungary "King of Hungary"), [Saint Stephen](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Stephen_I_of_Hungary "Stephen I of Hungary") and his son [Saint Emeric](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Emeric_of_Hungary "Saint Emeric of Hungary") celebrated as a National Day in Hungary.

## Year - 917
[Battle of Acheloos](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Achelous_(917) "Battle of Achelous (917)"): Tsar [Simeon I of Bulgaria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Simeon_I_of_Bulgaria "Simeon I of Bulgaria") decisively defeats a [Byzantine](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Empire "Byzantine Empire") army.

## Year - 636
[Battle of Yarmouk](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Yarmouk "Battle of Yarmouk"): [Arab](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabs "Arabs") forces led by [Khalid ibn al-Walid](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Khalid_ibn_al-Walid "Khalid ibn al-Walid") take control of the [Levant](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Levant "Levant") away from the [Byzantine Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Empire "Byzantine Empire"), marking the first great wave of [Muslim](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Muslim "Muslim") conquests and the rapid advance of [Islam](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Islam "Islam") outside [Arabia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabian_Peninsula "Arabian Peninsula").

## Year - 14
D 14 – [Agrippa Postumus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Agrippa_Postumus "Agrippa Postumus"), maternal grandson of the late [Roman emperor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_emperor "Roman emperor") [Augustus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Augustus "Augustus"), is mysteriously executed by his guards while in exile.
