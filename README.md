## Year - 2005
 [Kuwait](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuwait "Kuwait") permits [women's suffrage](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Women%27s_suffrage_in_Kuwait "Women's suffrage in Kuwait") in a 35-23 [National Assembly](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Assembly_(Kuwait) "National Assembly (Kuwait)") vote.

## Year - 2003
 In [Morocco](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Morocco "Morocco"), 33 civilians are killed and more than 100 people are injured in the [Casablanca terrorist attacks](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2003_Casablanca_bombings "2003 Casablanca bombings").

## Year - 1997
 [Mobutu Sese Seko](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mobutu_Sese_Seko "Mobutu Sese Seko"), the President of [Zaire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Zaire "Zaire"), flees the country.

## Year - 1991
 Queen [Elizabeth II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Elizabeth_II "Elizabeth II") of the United Kingdom addresses a joint session of the [United States Congress](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Congress "United States Congress"). She is the first British monarch to address the U.S. Congress.

## Year - 1988

## Year - 1975
 [Junko Tabei](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Junko_Tabei "Junko Tabei") from [Japan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "Japan") becomes the first woman to reach the summit of [Mount Everest](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mount_Everest "Mount Everest").

## Year - 1974
 [Josip Broz Tito](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Josip_Broz_Tito "Josip Broz Tito") is elected [president for life](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/President_for_life "President for life") of [Yugoslavia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Socialist_Federal_Republic_of_Yugoslavia "Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia").

## Year - 1969
 [Venera program](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Venera_program "Venera program"): <i>[Venera 5](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Venera_5 "Venera 5")</i>, a Soviet [space probe](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_probe "Space probe"), lands on [Venus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Venus "Venus").

## Year - 1966
 The [Chinese Communist Party](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_Communist_Party "Chinese Communist Party") issues the "[May 16 Notice](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/May_16_Notice "May 16 Notice")", marking the beginning of the [Cultural Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cultural_Revolution "Cultural Revolution").

## Year - 1960
 [Theodore Maiman](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Theodore_Maiman "Theodore Maiman") operates the first [optical laser](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Optical_laser "Optical laser") (a [ruby laser](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ruby_laser "Ruby laser")), at [Hughes Research Laboratories](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hughes_Research_Laboratories "Hughes Research Laboratories") in [Malibu, California](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Malibu,_California "Malibu, California").

## Year - 1959
 The [Tritons' Fountain](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tritons%27_Fountain "Tritons' Fountain") in [Valletta, Malta](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Valletta "Valletta") is turned on for the first time.

## Year - 1954
 Beginning of the [Kengir uprising](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kengir_uprising "Kengir uprising") in the [Gulag](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulag "Gulag").

## Year - 1951
 The first regularly scheduled [transatlantic flights](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Transatlantic_flight "Transatlantic flight") begin between Idlewild Airport (now [John F Kennedy International Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/John_F_Kennedy_International_Airport "John F Kennedy International Airport")) in New York City and [Heathrow Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Heathrow_Airport "Heathrow Airport") in London, operated by [El Al Israel Airlines](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Al_Israel_Airlines "El Al Israel Airlines").

## Year - 1945
 Beginning of the [Levant Crisis](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Levant_Crisis "Levant Crisis") between Britain and France in Syria. The latter try to quell nationalist protests but backs down after threat of military action by the British.

## Year - 1943
 [Operation Chastise](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Chastise "Operation Chastise") is undertaken by RAF Bomber Command with specially equipped [Avro Lancasters](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Avro_Lancaster "Avro Lancaster") to destroy the Mohne, Sorpe, and Eder dams in the Ruhr valley.

## Year - 1943
 [The Holocaust](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Holocaust "The Holocaust"): The [Warsaw Ghetto Uprising](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Warsaw_Ghetto_Uprising "Warsaw Ghetto Uprising") ends.

## Year - 1929
 In [Hollywood](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hollywood,_Los_Angeles "Hollywood, Los Angeles"), the [first](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1st_Academy_Awards "1st Academy Awards") [Academy Awards](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Academy_Awards "Academy Awards") ceremony takes place.

## Year - 1925
 The first modern performance of [Claudio Monteverdi](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Claudio_Monteverdi "Claudio Monteverdi")'s opera <i>[Il ritorno d'Ulisse in patria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Il_ritorno_d%27Ulisse_in_patria "Il ritorno d'Ulisse in patria")</i> occurred in [Paris](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Paris "Paris").

## Year - 1920
 In Rome, [Pope Benedict XV](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Benedict_XV "Pope Benedict XV") [canonizes Joan of Arc](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Canonization_of_Joan_of_Arc "Canonization of Joan of Arc").

## Year - 1919
 A naval [Curtiss NC-4](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Curtiss_NC-4 "Curtiss NC-4") aircraft commanded by [Albert Cushing Read](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Albert_Cushing_Read "Albert Cushing Read") leaves [Trepassey](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Trepassey "Trepassey"), [Newfoundland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Newfoundland_and_Labrador "Newfoundland and Labrador"), for [Lisbon](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lisbon "Lisbon") via the [Azores](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Azores "Azores") on the first [transatlantic flight](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Transatlantic_flight "Transatlantic flight").

## Year - 1918
 The [Sedition Act of 1918](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sedition_Act_of_1918 "Sedition Act of 1918") is passed by the U.S. Congress, making criticism of the government during wartime an imprisonable offense. It will be repealed less than two years later.

## Year - 1916
 The [United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom_of_Great_Britain_and_Ireland "United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland") and the [French Third Republic](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Third_Republic "French Third Republic") sign the secret wartime [Sykes-Picot Agreement](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sykes-Picot_Agreement "Sykes-Picot Agreement") [partitioning former Ottoman territories](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Partitioning_of_the_Ottoman_Empire "Partitioning of the Ottoman Empire") such as [Iraq](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Iraq "Iraq") and [Syria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Syria "Syria").

## Year - 1891
 The [International Electrotechnical Exhibition](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Electrotechnical_Exhibition "International Electrotechnical Exhibition") opened in [Frankfurt](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Frankfurt "Frankfurt"), Germany, featuring the world's first long-distance transmission of high-power, [three-phase electric current](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Three-phase_electric_power "Three-phase electric power") (the most common form today).

## Year - 1888
 [Nikola Tesla](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nikola_Tesla "Nikola Tesla") delivers a lecture describing the equipment which will allow efficient generation and use of [alternating currents](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alternating_current "Alternating current") to [transmit electric power](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Electric_power_transmission "Electric power transmission") over long distances.

## Year - 1877
 The [16 May 1877 crisis](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/16_May_1877_crisis "16 May 1877 crisis") occurs in [France](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Third_Republic "French Third Republic"), ending with the dissolution of the [National Assembly](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Parliament "French Parliament") 22 June and affirming the interpretation of the [Constitution of 1875](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Constitutional_Laws_of_1875 "French Constitutional Laws of 1875") as a [parliamentary](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Parliamentary "Parliamentary") rather than [presidential system](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Presidential_system "Presidential system"). The elections held in October 1877 led to the defeat of the [royalists](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Monarchism_in_France "Monarchism in France") as a formal political movement in France.

## Year - 1874
 A flood on the [Mill River](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mill_River_(Northampton,_Massachusetts) "Mill River (Northampton, Massachusetts)") in [Massachusetts](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Massachusetts "Massachusetts") destroys much of four villages and kills 139 people.

## Year - 1868
 The [United States Senate](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Senate "United States Senate") fails to [convict](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Impeachment_of_Andrew_Johnson "Impeachment of Andrew Johnson") President [Andrew Johnson](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Andrew_Johnson "Andrew Johnson") by one vote.

## Year - 1866
 The [United States Congress](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Congress "United States Congress") establishes the [nickel](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nickel_(United_States_coin) "Nickel (United States coin)").

## Year - 1842
 The first major [wagon train](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wagon_train "Wagon train") heading for the [Pacific Northwest](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Northwest "Pacific Northwest") sets out on the [Oregon Trail](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Oregon_Trail "Oregon Trail") from [Elm Grove, Missouri](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Elm_Grove,_Missouri "Elm Grove, Missouri"), with 100 pioneers.

## Year - 1834
 The [Battle of Asseiceira](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Asseiceira "Battle of Asseiceira") is fought; it was the final and decisive engagement of the [Liberal Wars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Liberal_Wars "Liberal Wars") in Portugal.

## Year - 1822
 [Greek War of Independence](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Greek_War_of_Independence "Greek War of Independence"): The [Turks](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey "Turkey") capture the Greek town of [Souli](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Souli "Souli").

## Year - 1812
 [Imperial Russia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Imperial_Russia "Imperial Russia") signs the [Treaty of Bucharest](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Bucharest_(1812) "Treaty of Bucharest (1812)"), ending the [Russo-Turkish War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Russo-Turkish_War_(1806%E2%80%9312) "Russo-Turkish War (1806-12)"). The [Ottoman Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ottoman_Empire "Ottoman Empire") cedes [Bessarabia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bessarabia "Bessarabia") to Russia.

## Year - 1811
 [Peninsular War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Peninsular_War "Peninsular War"): The allies Spain, Portugal and United Kingdom fight an inconclusive battle against the French at the [Albuera](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Albuera "Battle of Albuera"). It is, in proportion to the numbers involved, the bloodiest battle of the war.

## Year - 1771
 The [Battle of Alamance](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Alamance "Battle of Alamance"), a pre-[American Revolutionary War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Revolution "American Revolution") battle between local [militia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Militia "Militia") and a group of rebels called The "[Regulators](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_the_Regulation "War of the Regulation")", occurs in present-day [Alamance County, North Carolina](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alamance_County,_North_Carolina "Alamance County, North Carolina").

## Year - 1770
 The 14-year-old [Marie Antoinette](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Marie_Antoinette "Marie Antoinette") marries 15-year-old [Louis-Auguste](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_XVI_of_France "Louis XVI of France"), who later becomes king of [France](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Early_modern_France "Early modern France").

## Year - 1739
 The [Battle of Vasai](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Vasai "Battle of Vasai") concludes as the [Marathas](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Maratha_Empire "Maratha Empire") defeat the [Portuguese](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Portuguese_Empire "Portuguese Empire") army.

## Year - 1584
 [Santiago de Vera](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Santiago_de_Vera "Santiago de Vera") becomes sixth Governor-General of the Spanish colony of the [Philippines](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines").

## Year - 1568
 [Mary, Queen of Scots](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mary,_Queen_of_Scots "Mary, Queen of Scots"), flees to England.

## Year - 1532
 Sir [Thomas More](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas_More "Thomas More") resigns as [Lord Chancellor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lord_Chancellor "Lord Chancellor") of [England](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_England "Kingdom of England").

## Year - 1527
 The Florentines drive out the [Medici](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Medici "Medici") for a second time and [Florence](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Florence "Florence") re-establishes itself as [a republic](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Florence "Republic of Florence").

## Year - 1426
 Gov. [Thado of Mohnyin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mohnyin_Thado "Mohnyin Thado") becomes king of [Ava](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ava_Kingdom "Ava Kingdom").

## Year - 1364
 [Hundred Years' War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hundred_Years%27_War "Hundred Years' War"): [Bertrand du Guesclin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bertrand_du_Guesclin "Bertrand du Guesclin") and a French army defeat the Anglo-Navarrese army of [Charles the Bad](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_the_Bad "Charles the Bad") at [Cocherel](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Cocherel "Battle of Cocherel").

## Year - 1204
 [Baldwin IX, Count of Flanders](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Baldwin_I_of_Constantinople "Baldwin I of Constantinople") is crowned as the first [Emperor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor "Emperor") of the [Latin Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Latin_Empire "Latin Empire").

## Year - 946
[Emperor Suzaku](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor_Suzaku "Emperor Suzaku") abdicates the throne in favor of his brother [Murakami](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor_Murakami "Emperor Murakami") who becomes the 62nd emperor of [Japan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Japan "Japan").
