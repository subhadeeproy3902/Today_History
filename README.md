
# Today's History 📜

11th December 2024


## Year - 2020
 The [Food and Drug Administration](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Food_and_Drug_Administration "Food and Drug Administration") issues an [Emergency Use Authorization](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Emergency_Use_Authorization "Emergency Use Authorization") on the [Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pfizer%E2%80%93BioNTech_COVID-19_vaccine "Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine"), the first [COVID-19 vaccine](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/COVID-19_vaccine "COVID-19 vaccine") to be approved by the agency.

## Year - 2019
 The results of the [2019 Bougainvillean independence referendum](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2019_Bougainvillean_independence_referendum "2019 Bougainvillean independence referendum") are announced. The results are overwhelmingly one-sided. Over 98% of voters vote for [Bougainville](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Autonomous_Region_of_Bougainville "Autonomous Region of Bougainville")'s independence.

## Year - 2017
 [New York City Subway bombing](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2017_New_York_City_Subway_bombing "2017 New York City Subway bombing"): A pipe bomb partially detonates in the [New York City Subway](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City_Subway "New York City Subway"), in the [Times Square-42nd Street/Port Authority Bus Terminal](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Times_Square%E2%80%9342nd_Street/Port_Authority_Bus_Terminal_(New_York_City_Subway) "Times Square-42nd Street/Port Authority Bus Terminal (New York City Subway)"). Four people are injured, including the perpetrator.

## Year - 2012
 At least 125 people are killed and up to 200 injured in [bombings](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Aqrab_massacre "Aqrab massacre") in the [Alawite](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alawite "Alawite") village of [Aqrab](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Aqrab "Aqrab"), [Syria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Syria "Syria").

## Year - 2009
 Finnish game developer [Rovio Entertainment](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Rovio_Entertainment "Rovio Entertainment") releases the hit mobile game <i>[Angry Birds](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Angry_Birds_(video_game) "Angry Birds (video game)")</i> internationally on [iOS](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/IOS "IOS").

## Year - 2008
 [Bernie Madoff](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bernie_Madoff "Bernie Madoff") is arrested and charged with securities fraud in a $50 billion [Ponzi scheme](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ponzi_scheme "Ponzi scheme").

## Year - 2007
 [Insurgency in the Maghreb](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Insurgency_in_the_Maghreb_(2002%E2%80%93present) "Insurgency in the Maghreb (2002-present)"): [Two car bombs explode](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/December_11,_2007_Algiers_bombings "December 11, 2007 Algiers bombings") in [Algiers](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Algiers "Algiers"), [Algeria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Algeria "Algeria"), one near the Supreme Constitutional Court and the other near the offices of the United Nations.

## Year - 2006
 [Felipe Calderón](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Felipe_Calder%C3%B3n "Felipe Calderón"), the [President of Mexico](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Mexico "President of Mexico"), launches a [military-led offensive](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Michoac%C3%A1n "Operation Michoacán") to put down the drug cartel violence in the state of [Michoacán](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Michoac%C3%A1n "Michoacán"). This effort is often regarded as the first event in the [Mexican Drug War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexican_Drug_War "Mexican Drug War").

## Year - 2006
 The [International Conference to Review the Global Vision of the Holocaust](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Conference_to_Review_the_Global_Vision_of_the_Holocaust "International Conference to Review the Global Vision of the Holocaust") is opened in [Tehran](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tehran "Tehran"), [Iran](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran"), by then-president [Mahmoud Ahmadinejad](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahmoud_Ahmadinejad "Mahmoud Ahmadinejad"); nations such as Israel and the United States express concern.

## Year - 2005
 [Cronulla riots](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2005_Cronulla_riots "2005 Cronulla riots"): Thousands of White Australians demonstrate against ethnic violence resulting in a riot against anyone thought to be [Lebanese](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lebanon "Lebanon") in [Cronulla, New South Wales](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cronulla,_New_South_Wales "Cronulla, New South Wales"); these are followed up by retaliatory ethnic attacks on Cronulla.

## Year - 2005
 The [Buncefield Oil Depot catches fire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Buncefield_fire "Buncefield fire") in [Hemel Hempstead](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hemel_Hempstead "Hemel Hempstead"), England.

## Year - 2001
 China joins the [World Trade Organization](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Trade_Organization "World Trade Organization") (WTO).

## Year - 1999
 [SATA Air Açores Flight 530M](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/SATA_Air_A%C3%A7ores_Flight_530M "SATA Air Açores Flight 530M") crashes into [Pico da Esperança](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pico_da_Esperan%C3%A7a "Pico da Esperança") on [São Jorge Island](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Jorge_Island "São Jorge Island") in the [Azores](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Azores "Azores"), killing 35.

## Year - 1998
 [Thai Airways Flight 261 crashes](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Thai_Airways_International_Flight_261 "Thai Airways International Flight 261") near [Surat Thani Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Surat_Thani_Airport "Surat Thani Airport"), killing 101. The pilot flying the [Airbus A310-200](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Airbus_A310-200 "Airbus A310-200") is thought to have suffered [spatial disorientation](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Spatial_disorientation "Spatial disorientation").

## Year - 1997
 The [Kyoto Protocol](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kyoto_Protocol "Kyoto Protocol") opens for signature.

## Year - 1994
 A bomb explodes on [Philippine Airlines Flight 434](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine_Airlines_Flight_434 "Philippine Airlines Flight 434"), en route from [Manila](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Manila "Manila"), [Philippines](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"), to Tokyo, Japan, killing one. The captain is able to land the plane safely.

## Year - 1994
 [First Chechen War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Chechen_War "First Chechen War"): [Russian President](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Russia "President of Russia") [Boris Yeltsin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Boris_Yeltsin "Boris Yeltsin") orders Russian troops into [Chechnya](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chechnya "Chechnya").

## Year - 1993
 A block of the [Highland Towers](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Highland_Towers_collapse "Highland Towers collapse") [condominium](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Condominium_(living_space) "Condominium (living space)") complex collapses following a [landslide](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Landslide "Landslide") caused by heavy rain and water flowing from a construction site at [Ampang](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ampang,_Kuala_Lumpur "Ampang, Kuala Lumpur") district in [Kuala Lumpur](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuala_Lumpur "Kuala Lumpur"), Malaysia. 48 of its residents die, including one who died in hospital after being rescued alive, leaving only two survivors.

## Year - 1990
 Several fatal collisions in the [1990 Interstate 75 fog disaster](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1990_Interstate_75_fog_disaster "1990 Interstate 75 fog disaster") result in a total of 12 deaths and 42 being injured

## Year - 1990
 Demonstrations by students and workers across [Albania](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Albania "Albania") begin, which eventually trigger the [fall of communism in Albania](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Fall_of_communism_in_Albania "Fall of communism in Albania").

## Year - 1988
 A [Soviet Air Force](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Air_Forces "Soviet Air Forces") Il-76 aircraft [crashes](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1988_Soviet_Air_Force_Il-76_crash "1988 Soviet Air Force Il-76 crash") while participating in the [Armenian earthquake](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1988_Armenian_earthquake "1988 Armenian earthquake") relief, killing 78 people.

## Year - 1981
 [El Mozote massacre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Mozote_massacre "El Mozote massacre"): Armed forces in [El Salvador](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Salvador "El Salvador") kill an estimated 900 civilians in an anti-guerrilla campaign during the [Salvadoran Civil War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Salvadoran_Civil_War "Salvadoran Civil War").

## Year - 1980
 The Comprehensive Environmental Response, Compensation, and Liability Act ([Superfund](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Superfund "Superfund")) is enacted by the [U.S. Congress](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Congress "United States Congress").

## Year - 1978
 The [Lufthansa heist](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lufthansa_heist "Lufthansa heist") is committed by a group led by [Lucchese family](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lucchese_crime_family "Lucchese crime family") associate [Jimmy Burke](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Burke_(gangster) "James Burke (gangster)"). It was the largest cash robbery ever committed on American soil, at that time.

## Year - 1972
 [Apollo 17](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo_17 "Apollo 17") becomes the sixth and final [Apollo mission](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo_program "Apollo program") to land on the [Moon](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Moon "Moon").

## Year - 1964
 [Che Guevara](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Che_Guevara "Che Guevara") speaks at the [United Nations General Assembly](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_General_Assembly "United Nations General Assembly") in New York City.

## Year - 1962
 [Arthur Lucas](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Arthur_Lucas "Arthur Lucas"), convicted of murder, is the last person to be [executed in Canada](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_punishment_in_Canada "Capital punishment in Canada").

## Year - 1960
 French forces crack down in a violent clash with protesters in [French Algeria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Algeria "French Algeria") during a visit by [French President](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_France "President of France") [Charles de Gaulle](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_de_Gaulle "Charles de Gaulle").

## Year - 1958
 [French Upper Volta](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Upper_Volta "French Upper Volta") and [French Dahomey](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Dahomey "French Dahomey") gain self-government from [France](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Fourth_Republic "French Fourth Republic"), becoming the [Republic of Upper Volta](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Upper_Volta "Republic of Upper Volta") (now [Burkina Faso](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Burkina_Faso "Burkina Faso")) and the [Republic of Dahomey](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Dahomey "Republic of Dahomey") (now [Benin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Benin "Benin")), respectively, and joining the [French Community](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Community "French Community").

## Year - 1948
 [Arab-Israeli War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1948_Arab%E2%80%93Israeli_War "1948 Arab-Israeli War"): The [United Nations](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations "United Nations") passes [General Assembly Resolution 194](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_General_Assembly_Resolution_194 "United Nations General Assembly Resolution 194"), creating a [Conciliation Commission](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Conciliation_Commission "United Nations Conciliation Commission") to mediate the conflict.

## Year - 1946
 The United Nations International Children's Emergency Fund ([UNICEF](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/UNICEF "UNICEF")) is established.

## Year - 1941
 World War II: The [Imperial Japanese Navy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Imperial_Japanese_Navy "Imperial Japanese Navy") suffers its first loss of surface vessels during the [Battle of Wake Island](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Wake_Island "Battle of Wake Island").

## Year - 1941
 World War II: [Poland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Polish_government-in-exile "Polish government-in-exile") declares war on the [Empire of Japan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Empire_of_Japan "Empire of Japan").

## Year - 1941
 [World War II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II "World War II"): [Germany](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazi_Germany "Nazi Germany") and Italy declare war on the United States, following the Americans' declaration of war on the [Empire of Japan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Empire_of_Japan "Empire of Japan") in the wake of the [attack on Pearl Harbor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Attack_on_Pearl_Harbor "Attack on Pearl Harbor"). The United States, in turn, declares war on them.

## Year - 1937
 [Second Italo-Ethiopian War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Italo-Ethiopian_War "Second Italo-Ethiopian War"): [Italy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Italy "Kingdom of Italy") leaves the [League of Nations](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/League_of_Nations "League of Nations").

## Year - 1936
 [Abdication Crisis](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_VIII_abdication_crisis "Edward VIII abdication crisis"): [Edward VIII](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_VIII "Edward VIII")'s abdication as King of the United Kingdom and the [British Dominions beyond the Seas](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dominion "Dominion"), and [Emperor of India](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor_of_India "Emperor of India"), becomes effective.

## Year - 1934
 [Bill Wilson](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bill_W. "Bill W."), co-founder of [Alcoholics Anonymous](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alcoholics_Anonymous "Alcoholics Anonymous"), takes his last drink and enters treatment for the final time.

## Year - 1931
 [Statute of Westminster 1931](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Statute_of_Westminster_1931 "Statute of Westminster 1931"): The [British Parliament](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Parliament_of_the_United_Kingdom "Parliament of the United Kingdom") establishes legislative equality between the UK and the [Dominions](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dominion "Dominion") of the [Commonwealth](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Commonwealth_of_Nations "Commonwealth of Nations")—Australia, Canada, [Newfoundland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dominion_of_Newfoundland "Dominion of Newfoundland"), [New Zealand](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dominion_of_New_Zealand "Dominion of New Zealand"), [South Africa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Union_of_South_Africa "Union of South Africa"), and [Ireland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Irish_Free_State "Irish Free State").

## Year - 1927
 [Guangzhou Uprising](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Guangzhou_Uprising "Guangzhou Uprising"): [Communist](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Communist_Party_of_China "Communist Party of China") [Red Guards](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Guards_(China) "Red Guards (China)") launch an uprising in [Guangzhou](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Guangzhou "Guangzhou"), China, taking over most of the city and announcing the formation of a Guangzhou [Soviet](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_(council) "Soviet (council)").

## Year - 1925
 [Roman Catholic](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Catholic_Church "Catholic Church") [papal](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope "Pope") [encyclical](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Encyclical "Encyclical") <i>[Quas primas](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Quas_primas "Quas primas")</i> introduces the [Feast of Christ the King](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Feast_of_Christ_the_King "Feast of Christ the King").

## Year - 1920
 [Irish War of Independence](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Irish_War_of_Independence "Irish War of Independence"): In retaliation for a recent [IRA](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Irish_Republican_Army "Irish Republican Army") ambush, British forces [burn and loot numerous buildings in Cork city](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Burning_of_Cork "Burning of Cork"). Many civilians report being beaten, shot at, robbed and verbally abused by British forces.

## Year - 1917
 [World War I](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_I "World War I"): British General [Edmund Allenby](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Edmund_Allenby,_1st_Viscount_Allenby "Edmund Allenby, 1st Viscount Allenby") enters [Jerusalem](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jerusalem "Jerusalem") on foot and declares [martial law](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Martial_law "Martial law").

## Year - 1913
 More than two years after it was stolen from the [Louvre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Louvre "Louvre"), [Leonardo da Vinci](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Leonardo_da_Vinci "Leonardo da Vinci")'s painting <i>[Mona Lisa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mona_Lisa "Mona Lisa")</i> is recovered in [Florence](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Florence "Florence"), Italy. The thief, [Vincenzo Peruggia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vincenzo_Peruggia "Vincenzo Peruggia"), is immediately arrested.

## Year - 1907
 The [New Zealand Parliament Buildings](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand_Parliament_Buildings "New Zealand Parliament Buildings") are almost completely destroyed by fire.

## Year - 1905
 A workers' uprising occurs in [Kyiv](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kyiv "Kyiv"), Ukraine (then part of the [Russian Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Empire "Russian Empire")), and establishes the [Shuliavka Republic](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Shuliavka_Republic "Shuliavka Republic").

## Year - 1901
 [Guglielmo Marconi](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Guglielmo_Marconi "Guglielmo Marconi") transmits the first transatlantic radio signal from [Poldhu, Cornwall](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Poldhu,_Cornwall "Poldhu, Cornwall"), England to [Saint John's](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/St._John%27s,_Newfoundland_and_Labrador "St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador"), [Newfoundland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dominion_of_Newfoundland "Dominion of Newfoundland").

## Year - 1899
 [Second Boer War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Boer_War "Second Boer War"): In the [Battle of Magersfontein](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Magersfontein "Battle of Magersfontein") the [Boers](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Boer "Boer") commanded by general [Piet Cronjé](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Piet_Cronj%C3%A9 "Piet Cronjé") inflict a defeat on the forces of the [British Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Empire "British Empire") commanded by [Lord Methuen](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_Methuen,_3rd_Baron_Methuen "Paul Methuen, 3rd Baron Methuen") trying to relieve the [Siege of Kimberley](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Kimberley "Siege of Kimberley").

## Year - 1868
 [Paraguayan War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Paraguayan_War "Paraguayan War"): [Brazilian troops](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazilian_Army "Brazilian Army") defeat the [Paraguayan Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Paraguayan_Army "Paraguayan Army") at the [Battle of Avay](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Avay "Battle of Avay").

## Year - 1816
 [Indiana](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Indiana "Indiana") becomes the 19th [U.S. state](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/U.S._state "U.S. state").

## Year - 1815
 The [U.S. Senate](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Senate "United States Senate") creates a select committee on finance and a uniform national currency, predecessor of the [United States Senate Committee on Finance](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Senate_Committee_on_Finance "United States Senate Committee on Finance").

## Year - 1792
 [French Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Revolution "French Revolution"): King [Louis XVI of France](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_XVI_of_France "Louis XVI of France") [is put on trial for treason](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Trial_of_Louis_XVI "Trial of Louis XVI") by the [National Convention](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Convention "National Convention").

## Year - 1688
 [Glorious Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Glorious_Revolution "Glorious Revolution"): [James II of England](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/James_II_of_England "James II of England"), while trying to flee to France, throws the [Great Seal of the Realm](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Seal_of_the_Realm "Great Seal of the Realm") into the [River Thames](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/River_Thames "River Thames").

## Year - 1675
 [Antonio de Vea expedition](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Antonio_de_Vea_expedition "Antonio de Vea expedition") enters [San Rafael Lake](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Rafael_Lake "San Rafael Lake") in western Patagonia.

## Year - 1640
 The [Root and Branch petition](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Root_and_Branch_petition "Root and Branch petition"), signed by 15,000 Londoners calling for the abolition of the [episcopacy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Episcopal_polity "Episcopal polity"), is presented to the [Long Parliament](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Long_Parliament "Long Parliament").

## Year - 1602
 A surprise attack by forces under the command of [Charles Emmanuel I, Duke of Savoy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Emmanuel_I,_Duke_of_Savoy "Charles Emmanuel I, Duke of Savoy"), and his brother-in-law, [Philip III of Spain](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Philip_III_of_Spain "Philip III of Spain"), is repelled by the citizens of [Geneva](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Geneva "Geneva"). (Commemorated annually by the <i>[Fête de l'Escalade](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/L%27Escalade "L'Escalade")</i>.)

## Year - 1282
 [Battle of Orewin Bridge](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Orewin_Bridge "Battle of Orewin Bridge"): [Llywelyn ap Gruffudd](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Llywelyn_ap_Gruffudd "Llywelyn ap Gruffudd"), the last native [Prince of Wales](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_of_Wales "Prince of Wales"), is killed at [Cilmeri](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cilmeri "Cilmeri") near [Builth Wells](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Builth_Wells "Builth Wells") in [mid-Wales](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mid_Wales "Mid Wales").

## Year - 1239
 [Treaty of Benavente](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Benavente "Treaty of Benavente"): the heiresses of the [Kingdom of León](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Le%C3%B3n "Kingdom of León") renounce their throne to King [Ferdinand III of Castile](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ferdinand_III_of_Castile "Ferdinand III of Castile")

## Year - 1041
 [Michael V](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_V_Kalaphates "Michael V Kalaphates"), adoptive son of Empress [Zoë of Byzantium](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Zo%C3%AB_Porphyrogenita "Zoë Porphyrogenita"), is proclaimed emperor of the [Eastern Roman Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Empire "Byzantine Empire").

## Year - 969
[Byzantine Emperor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Emperor "Byzantine Emperor") [Nikephoros II Phokas](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nikephoros_II_Phokas "Nikephoros II Phokas") is assassinated by his wife [Theophano](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Theophano_(born_Anastaso) "Theophano (born Anastaso)") and her lover, the later Emperor [John I Tzimiskes](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/John_I_Tzimiskes "John I Tzimiskes").

## Year - 861
Assassination of the [Abbasid](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Abbasid "Abbasid") caliph [al-Mutawakkil](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Al-Mutawakkil "Al-Mutawakkil") by the Turkish guard, who raise [al-Muntasir](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Al-Muntasir "Al-Muntasir") to the throne, start of the "[Anarchy at Samarra](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Anarchy_at_Samarra "Anarchy at Samarra")".

## Year - 361
[Julian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Julian_(emperor) "Julian (emperor)") enters [Constantinople](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Constantinople "Constantinople") as sole [Roman Emperor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_emperor "Roman emperor").

## Year - 220
[Emperor Xian of Han](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor_Xian_of_Han "Emperor Xian of Han") is forced to abdicate the throne by [Cao Cao](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cao_Cao "Cao Cao")'s son [Cao Pi](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cao_Pi "Cao Pi"), [ending the Han dynasty](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/End_of_the_Han_dynasty "End of the Han dynasty").
