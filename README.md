
# Today's History 📜

2nd September 2025


## Year - 2024
 At least 129 inmates are killed and 59 more injured in an [attempted prison break](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2024_Makala_jailbreak_attempt "2024 Makala jailbreak attempt") at [Makala Prison](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Makala_Prison "Makala Prison") in [Kinshasa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kinshasa "Kinshasa"), [Democratic Republic of the Congo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo "Democratic Republic of the Congo").

## Year - 2024
 A [suicide bombing](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2024_Qala_Bakhtiar_bombing "2024 Qala Bakhtiar bombing") in [Kabul](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kabul "Kabul"), [Afghanistan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan"), kills 6 people and injures 13.

## Year - 2024
 Four people are killed in a [mass shooting](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2024_CTA_shooting "2024 CTA shooting") targeting homeless people on a Chicago Transit Authority train in Forest Park, [Illinois](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Illinois "Illinois"), [United States](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States").

## Year - 2023
 [India's first solar observation mission](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Aditya-L1 "Aditya-L1"): The [Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/ISRO "ISRO") successfully launches [Aditya-L1](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Aditya-L1 "Aditya-L1") from [Satish Dhawan space centre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Satish_Dhawan_Space_Centre "Satish Dhawan Space Centre").

## Year - 2022
 Eighteen people are killed and 23 others are injured by a [suicide bombing](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2022_Herat_mosque_bombing "2022 Herat mosque bombing") at a Sunni mosque in [Herat](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Herat "Herat"), [Afghanistan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan").

## Year - 2019
 The dive boat MV <i>Conception</i> [catches fire and sinks](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sinking_of_MV_Conception "Sinking of MV Conception") near [Santa Cruz Island](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Santa_Cruz_Island "Santa Cruz Island"), killing 34.

## Year - 2019
 [Hurricane Dorian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hurricane_Dorian "Hurricane Dorian"), a [category 5 hurricane](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Category_5_Hurricane "Category 5 Hurricane"), devastates [the Bahamas](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Bahamas "The Bahamas"), killing at least five.

## Year - 2018
 [National Museum of Brazil fire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Museum_of_Brazil_fire "National Museum of Brazil fire"), A massive fire destroys most of the [Paço de São Cristóvão](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pa%C3%A7o_de_S%C3%A3o_Crist%C3%B3v%C3%A3o "Paço de São Cristóvão"), which houses the National Museum of Brazil, in [Rio de Janeiro](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro "Rio de Janeiro"). The museum holds important archaeοlogical and anthropological objects, including the remains of the Luzia Woman, Marajoara vases and Egyptian mummies.

## Year - 2013
 The [Eastern span replacement of the San Francisco-Oakland Bay Bridge](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Eastern_span_replacement_of_the_San_Francisco%E2%80%93Oakland_Bay_Bridge "Eastern span replacement of the San Francisco-Oakland Bay Bridge") opens at 10:15 pm at a cost of $6.4 billion, after the [1989 Loma Prieta earthquake](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1989_Loma_Prieta_earthquake "1989 Loma Prieta earthquake") damaged the old span.

## Year - 2010
 [Israel-Palestinian conflict](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli%E2%80%93Palestinian_conflict "Israeli-Palestinian conflict"): the [2010 Israeli-Palestinian peace talks](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2010%E2%80%9311_Israeli%E2%80%93Palestinian_peace_talks "2010-11 Israeli-Palestinian peace talks") are launched by the United States.

## Year - 2009
 The [Andhra Pradesh, India helicopter crash](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2009_Andhra_Pradesh_Chief_Minister_helicopter_crash "2009 Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister helicopter crash") occurred near Rudrakonda Hill, 40 nautical miles (74 km) from [Kurnool](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kurnool "Kurnool"), [Andhra Pradesh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Andhra_Pradesh "Andhra Pradesh"), [India](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India"). Fatalities included [Y. S. Rajasekhara Reddy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Y._S._Rajasekhara_Reddy "Y. S. Rajasekhara Reddy"), the Chief Minister of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.

## Year - 2008
 [Google](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Google "Google") launches its [Google Chrome](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Google_Chrome "Google Chrome") web browser.

## Year - 1998
 The [UN](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations "United Nations")'s [International Criminal Tribunal for Rwanda](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Criminal_Tribunal_for_Rwanda "International Criminal Tribunal for Rwanda") finds [Jean-Paul Akayesu](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean-Paul_Akayesu "Jean-Paul Akayesu"), the former [mayor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mayor "Mayor") of a small town in [Rwanda](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Rwanda "Rwanda"), guilty of nine counts of [genocide](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Genocide "Genocide").

## Year - 1998
 [Swissair Flight 111](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Swissair_Flight_111 "Swissair Flight 111") crashes near [Peggy's Cove, Nova Scotia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Peggy%27s_Cove,_Nova_Scotia "Peggy's Cove, Nova Scotia"); all 229 people on board are killed.

## Year - 1992
 The 7.7 M<sub>w</sub>  [Nicaragua earthquake](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1992_Nicaragua_earthquake "1992 Nicaragua earthquake") affected the west coast of Nicaragua. With a <a href="https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Seismic_magnitude_scales#Ms" title="Seismic magnitude scales"><style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1038841319">.mw-parser-output .tooltip-dotted{border-bottom:1px dotted;cursor:help}</style>
<span class="rt-commentedText tooltip" title="Surface-wave mag.">M<sub>s</sub></span></a>-<a href="https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Seismic_magnitude_scales#Mw" title="Seismic magnitude scales"><link rel="mw-deduplicated-inline-style" href="mw-data:TemplateStyles:r1038841319">
<span class="rt-commentedText tooltip" title="Moment mag. scale">M<sub>w</sub></span></a> disparity of half a unit, this [tsunami earthquake](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tsunami_earthquake "Tsunami earthquake") triggered a tsunami that caused most of the damage and casualties, with at least 116 killed. Typical runup heights were 3-8 meters (9.8-26.2 ft).

## Year - 1990
 [Transnistria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Transnistria "Transnistria") is unilaterally proclaimed a [Soviet](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union") republic; the [Soviet president](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Presidium_of_the_Supreme_Soviet "Presidium of the Supreme Soviet") [Mikhail Gorbachev](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mikhail_Gorbachev "Mikhail Gorbachev") declares the decision null and void.

## Year - 1987
 In Moscow, the trial begins for 19-year-old pilot [Mathias Rust](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathias_Rust "Mathias Rust"), who flew his [Cessna](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cessna "Cessna") airplane into [Red Square](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Square "Red Square") in May.

## Year - 1985
 [Sri Lankan Civil War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sri_Lankan_civil_war "Sri Lankan civil war"): Sri Lankan Tamil politicians and former MPs [M. Alalasundaram](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/M._Alalasundaram "M. Alalasundaram") and [V. Dharmalingam](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/V._Dharmalingam "V. Dharmalingam") are shot dead.

## Year - 1984
 Seven people are shot and killed and 12 wounded in the [Milperra massacre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Milperra_massacre "Milperra massacre"), a shootout between the rival motorcycle [gangs](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gang "Gang") [Bandidos](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bandidos_Motorcycle_Club "Bandidos Motorcycle Club") and [Comancheros](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Comanchero_Motorcycle_Club "Comanchero Motorcycle Club") in Sydney, Australia.

## Year - 1970
 [Aeroflot Flight 3630](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Aeroflot_Flight_3630 "Aeroflot Flight 3630"), a [Tupolev Tu-124](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tupolev_Tu-124 "Tupolev Tu-124") en route from southern Russia to [Lithuania](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lithuania "Lithuania"), crashes after the pilots lost control of the aircraft at cruise altitude between [Rostov-on-Don Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Rostov-on-Don_Airport "Rostov-on-Don Airport") and [Vilnius Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vilnius_Airport "Vilnius Airport"), on the second leg of the flight; all 37 passengers and crew are killed.

## Year - 1970
 [NASA](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/NASA "NASA") announces the [cancellation](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Canceled_Apollo_missions "Canceled Apollo missions") of two [Apollo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo_program "Apollo program") missions to the Moon, [Apollo 15](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo_15 "Apollo 15") (the designation is re-used by a later mission), and [Apollo 19](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Canceled_Apollo_missions#Apollos_18-20 "Canceled Apollo missions").

## Year - 1968
 [Operation OAU](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_OAU "Operation OAU") begins during the [Nigerian Civil War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigerian_Civil_War "Nigerian Civil War").

## Year - 1963
 <i>[CBS Evening News](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/CBS_Evening_News "CBS Evening News")</i> becomes U.S. network television's first half-hour weeknight news broadcast, when the show is lengthened from 15 to 30 minutes.

## Year - 1960
 The first election of the [Tibetan Parliament-in-Exile](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Parliament_of_the_Central_Tibetan_Administration "Parliament of the Central Tibetan Administration"). The Tibetan community observes this date as Democracy Day.

## Year - 1958
 A USAF [RC-130](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lockheed_RC-130_Hercules "Lockheed RC-130 Hercules") [is shot down](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1958_C-130_shootdown_incident "1958 C-130 shootdown incident") by fighters over Armenia when it strays into Soviet airspace while conducting a [sigint](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Signals_intelligence "Signals intelligence") mission. All crew members are killed.

## Year - 1957
 President [Ngô Đình Diệm](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ng%C3%B4_%C4%90%C3%ACnh_Di%E1%BB%87m "Ngô Đình Diệm") of [South Vietnam](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Vietnam "South Vietnam") becomes [the first foreign head of state to make a state visit to Australia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ngo_Dinh_Diem_presidential_visit_to_Australia "Ngo Dinh Diem presidential visit to Australia").

## Year - 1946
 The [Interim Government of India](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Interim_Government_of_India "Interim Government of India") is formed, headed by [Jawaharlal Nehru](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jawaharlal_Nehru "Jawaharlal Nehru") as vice president with the powers of a Prime Minister.

## Year - 1945
 Communist leader [Ho Chi Minh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ho_Chi_Minh "Ho Chi Minh") [proclaimed](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Proclamation_of_Independence_of_the_Democratic_Republic_of_Vietnam "Proclamation of Independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam") the [Democratic Republic of Vietnam](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Vietnam "North Vietnam") after the end of the [Nguyễn dynasty](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nguy%E1%BB%85n_dynasty "Nguyễn dynasty").

## Year - 1945
 World War II: The [Japanese Instrument of Surrender](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_Instrument_of_Surrender "Japanese Instrument of Surrender") is signed by Japan and the major warring powers aboard the battleship [USS <i>Missouri</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Missouri_(BB-63) "USS Missouri (BB-63)") in [Tokyo Bay](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tokyo_Bay "Tokyo Bay").

## Year - 1944
 The last execution of a Finn in [Finland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Finland "Finland") takes place when soldier [Olavi Laiho](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Olavi_Laiho "Olavi Laiho") is executed by shooting in [Oulu](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Oulu "Oulu").

## Year - 1939
 [World War II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II "World War II"): Following the start of the [invasion of Poland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Invasion_of_Poland "Invasion of Poland") the previous day, the [Free City of Danzig](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_City_of_Danzig "Free City of Danzig") (now [Gdańsk](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gda%C5%84sk "Gdańsk"), Poland) is annexed by [Nazi Germany](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazi_Germany "Nazi Germany").

## Year - 1935
 The [Labor Day Hurricane](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1935_Labor_Day_Hurricane "1935 Labor Day Hurricane"), the most intense hurricane to strike the United States, makes landfall at [Long Key](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Long_Key "Long Key"), Florida, killing at least 400.

## Year - 1923
 [Kantō Massacre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kant%C5%8D_Massacre "Kantō Massacre"): Amid rumors that Koreans had been conducting acts of sabotage in the aftermath of the [1923 Great Kantō earthquake](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1923_Great_Kant%C5%8D_earthquake "1923 Great Kantō earthquake"), lynch mobs of Japanese begin massacring thousands of civilians over the course of several weeks, mainly ethnic minorities such as [Koreans](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Zainichi_Koreans "Zainichi Koreans") and Chinese.

## Year - 1912
 [Arthur Rose Eldred](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Arthur_Rose_Eldred "Arthur Rose Eldred") is awarded the first [Eagle Scout](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Eagle_Scout_(Boy_Scouts_of_America) "Eagle Scout (Boy Scouts of America)") award of the [Boy Scouts of America](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Boy_Scouts_of_America "Boy Scouts of America").

## Year - 1901
 Vice President of the United States [Theodore Roosevelt](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Theodore_Roosevelt "Theodore Roosevelt") utters the famous phrase, "Speak softly and carry a [big stick](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_Stick_ideology "Big Stick ideology")" at the [Minnesota State Fair](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Minnesota_State_Fair "Minnesota State Fair").

## Year - 1898
 [Battle of Omdurman](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Omdurman "Battle of Omdurman"): British and Egyptian troops defeat [Sudanese](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan "Sudan") tribesmen and establish British dominance in Sudan.

## Year - 1885
 [Rock Springs massacre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Rock_Springs_massacre "Rock Springs massacre"): In [Rock Springs, Wyoming](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Rock_Springs,_Wyoming "Rock Springs, Wyoming"), 150 white miners, who are struggling to unionize so they could strike for better wages and work conditions, attack their [Chinese](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Overseas_Chinese "Overseas Chinese") fellow workers killing 28, wounding 15 and forcing several hundred more out of town.

## Year - 1870
 [Franco-Prussian War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Franco-Prussian_War "Franco-Prussian War"): [Battle of Sedan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Sedan "Battle of Sedan"): [Prussian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prussia "Prussia") forces take [Napoleon III of France](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Napoleon_III_of_France "Napoleon III of France") and 100,000 of his soldiers prisoner.

## Year - 1867
 Mutsuhito, [Emperor Meiji](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor_Meiji "Emperor Meiji") of Japan, marries Masako Ichijō, thereafter known as [Empress Shōken](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Empress_Sh%C5%8Dken "Empress Shōken").

## Year - 1864
 American Civil War: Union forces enter [Atlanta](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlanta "Atlanta"), a day after the [Confederate](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America "Confederate States of America") defenders flee the city, ending the [Atlanta Campaign](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlanta_Campaign "Atlanta Campaign").

## Year - 1862
 [American Civil War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Civil_War "American Civil War"): United States President Abraham Lincoln reluctantly restores Union General [George B. McClellan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/George_B._McClellan "George B. McClellan") to full command after General [John Pope](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Pope_(general) "John Pope (general)")'s disastrous defeat at the [Second Battle of Bull Run](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Battle_of_Bull_Run "Second Battle of Bull Run").

## Year - 1859
 The [Carrington Event](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Carrington_Event "Carrington Event") is the strongest geomagnetic storm on record.

## Year - 1856
 The [Tianjing incident](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tianjing_incident "Tianjing incident") takes place in [Nanjing](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nanjing "Nanjing"), China.

## Year - 1807
 Napoleonic Wars: The British Royal Navy [bombards Copenhagen](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Copenhagen_(1807) "Battle of Copenhagen (1807)") with fire bombs and phosphorus rockets to prevent Denmark from surrendering its fleet to Napoleon.

## Year - 1806
 A massive landslide destroys the town of [Goldau](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Goldau "Goldau"), Switzerland, killing 457.

## Year - 1792
 During what became known as the [September Massacres](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/September_Massacres "September Massacres") of the French Revolution, rampaging mobs slaughter three Roman Catholic bishops, more than two hundred priests, and prisoners believed to be royalist sympathizers.

## Year - 1789
 The [United States Department of the Treasury](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Department_of_the_Treasury "United States Department of the Treasury") is founded.

## Year - 1752
 [Great Britain](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Great_Britain "Kingdom of Great Britain"), along with its [overseas possessions](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Empire "British Empire"), [adopts the Gregorian calendar](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Adoption_of_the_Gregorian_calendar#Great_Britain_and_its_colonies "Adoption of the Gregorian calendar").

## Year - 1666
 The [Great Fire of London](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Fire_of_London "Great Fire of London") breaks out and burns for three days, destroying 10,000 buildings, including [Old St Paul's Cathedral](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_St_Paul%27s_Cathedral "Old St Paul's Cathedral").

## Year - 1649
 The Italian city of [Castro](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Castro,_Lazio "Castro, Lazio") is completely destroyed by the forces of [Pope Innocent X](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Innocent_X "Pope Innocent X"), ending the [Wars of Castro](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wars_of_Castro "Wars of Castro").

## Year - 1601
 [4th Spanish Armada](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/4th_Spanish_Armada "4th Spanish Armada") makes landfall in Ireland at [Kinsale](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kinsale "Kinsale").

## Year - 1561
 [Entry of Mary, Queen of Scots into Edinburgh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Entry_of_Mary,_Queen_of_Scots_into_Edinburgh "Entry of Mary, Queen of Scots into Edinburgh"), a spectacular civic celebration for the Queen of Scotland, marred by religious controversy.

## Year - 1192
 The [Treaty of Jaffa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Jaffa_(1192) "Treaty of Jaffa (1192)") is signed between [Richard I of England](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_I_of_England "Richard I of England") and [Saladin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Saladin "Saladin"), leading to the end of the [Third Crusade](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Third_Crusade "Third Crusade").

## Year - 31 BC
- 31 BC - [Final War of the Roman Republic](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Final_War_of_the_Roman_Republic "Final War of the Roman Republic"): [Battle of Actium](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Actium "Battle of Actium"): Off the western coast of Greece, forces of [Octavian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Augustus "Augustus") defeat troops under [Mark Antony](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mark_Antony "Mark Antony") and [Cleopatra](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cleopatra "Cleopatra").

## Year - 44
4 BC - [Cicero](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cicero "Cicero") launches the first of his <i>[Philippicae](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippicae "Philippicae")</i> (oratorical attacks) on [Mark Antony](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mark_Antony "Mark Antony"). He will make 14 of them over the following months.

## Year - 44 BC
- 44 BC - [Pharaoh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pharaoh "Pharaoh") [Cleopatra VII](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cleopatra_VII "Cleopatra VII") of [Egypt](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Egypt "Egypt") declares her son co-ruler as Ptolemy XV [Caesarion](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Caesarion "Caesarion").
