## Year - 2015
 At least seven people are killed and many injured after gunmen [attack](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Gurdaspur_attack "2015 Gurdaspur attack") an Indian police station in [Punjab](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Punjab,_India "Punjab, India").

## Year - 2005
 After an incident during [STS-114](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/STS-114 "STS-114"), [NASA](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/NASA "NASA") grounds the [Space Shuttle](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_Shuttle "Space Shuttle"), pending an investigation of the continuing problem with the shedding of foam insulation from the [external fuel tank](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_Shuttle_external_tank "Space Shuttle external tank").

## Year - 2002
 [Ukraine airshow disaster](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sknyliv_air_show_disaster "Sknyliv air show disaster"): A [Sukhoi Su-27](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sukhoi_Su-27 "Sukhoi Su-27") fighter crashes during an air show at [Lviv](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lviv "Lviv"), [Ukraine](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ukraine "Ukraine") killing 77 and injuring more than 500 others, making it the deadliest air show disaster in history.

## Year - 1997

## Year - 1996
 In [Atlanta, United States](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlanta "Atlanta"), a [pipe bomb explodes at Centennial Olympic Park](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Centennial_Olympic_Park_bombing "Centennial Olympic Park bombing") during the [1996 Summer Olympics](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1996_Summer_Olympics "1996 Summer Olympics").

## Year - 1995
 The [Korean War Veterans Memorial](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Korean_War_Veterans_Memorial "Korean War Veterans Memorial") is dedicated in [Washington, D.C.](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington,_D.C. "Washington, D.C.")

## Year - 1990
 The [Supreme Soviet](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Soviet "Supreme Soviet") of the [Belarusian Soviet Republic](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Byelorussian_Soviet_Socialist_Republic "Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic") declares independence of [Belarus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Belarus "Belarus") from the [Soviet Union](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union"). Until [1996](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1996 "1996") the day is celebrated as the Independence Day of Belarus; after a [referendum](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1996_Belarusian_referendum "1996 Belarusian referendum") held that year the celebration of [independence](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Independence "Independence") is moved to [June 3](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/June_3 "June 3").

## Year - 1989
 While attempting to land at [Tripoli International Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tripoli_International_Airport "Tripoli International Airport") in Libya, [Korean Air Flight 803](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Korean_Air_Flight_803 "Korean Air Flight 803") crashes just short of the runway. Seventy-five of the 199 passengers and crew and four people on the ground are killed, in the second accident involving a [DC-10](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/DC-10 "DC-10") in less than two weeks, the first being [United Airlines Flight 232](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Airlines_Flight_232 "United Airlines Flight 232").

## Year - 1983
 [Black July](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_July "Black July"): Eighteen [Tamil](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sri_Lankan_Tamil_people "Sri Lankan Tamil people") political prisoners at the [Welikada](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Welikada "Welikada") [high security prison](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Welikada_Prison "Welikada Prison") in [Colombo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Colombo "Colombo") are [massacred](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Welikada_prison_massacre "Welikada prison massacre") by [Sinhalese](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sinhalese_people "Sinhalese people") prisoners, the second such massacre in two days.

## Year - 1981
 While landing at [Chihuahua International Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chihuahua_International_Airport "Chihuahua International Airport"), [Aeromexico Flight 230](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Aeromexico_Flight_230 "Aeromexico Flight 230") [overshoots the runway](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Runway_excursion "Runway excursion"). Thirty-two of the 66 passengers and crew on board the [DC-9](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/McDonnell_Douglas_DC-9 "McDonnell Douglas DC-9") are killed.

## Year - 1975
 [Mayor of Jaffna](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mayor_of_Jaffna "Mayor of Jaffna") and former MP [Alfred Duraiappah](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfred_Duraiappah "Alfred Duraiappah") is shot dead.

## Year - 1974
 [Watergate scandal](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Watergate_scandal "Watergate scandal"): The [House of Representatives](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_House_of_Representatives "United States House of Representatives") [Judiciary Committee](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_House_Committee_on_the_Judiciary "United States House Committee on the Judiciary") votes 27 to 11 to recommend the first article of [impeachment](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Impeachment "Impeachment") (for obstruction of justice) against [President](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States "President of the United States") [Richard Nixon](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Nixon "Richard Nixon").

## Year - 1964
 [Vietnam War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam_War "Vietnam War"): Five thousand more American military advisers are sent to [South Vietnam](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Vietnam "South Vietnam") bringing the total number of United States forces in [Vietnam](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam "Vietnam") to 21,000.

## Year - 1963
 The [Puijo observation tower](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Puijo_tower "Puijo tower") is opened to the general public at [Puijo Hill](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Puijo_Hill "Puijo Hill") in [Kuopio, Finland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuopio "Kuopio").

## Year - 1959
 The [Continental League](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Continental_League "Continental League") is announced as baseball's "third major league" in the United States.

## Year - 1955
 [El Al Flight 402](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Al_Flight_402 "El Al Flight 402") is shot down by two fighter jets after straying into Bulgarian air space. All 58 people on board are killed.

## Year - 1955
 The [Austrian State Treaty](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Austrian_State_Treaty "Austrian State Treaty") restores Austrian sovereignty.

## Year - 1953
 Cessation of hostilities is achieved in the [Korean War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Korean_War "Korean War") when the United States, China, and North Korea [sign an armistice agreement](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Korean_Armistice_Agreement "Korean Armistice Agreement"). [Syngman Rhee](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Syngman_Rhee "Syngman Rhee"), [President of South Korea](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_South_Korea "President of South Korea"), refuses to sign but pledges to observe the armistice.

## Year - 1949
 Initial flight of the [de Havilland Comet](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/De_Havilland_Comet "De Havilland Comet"), the first jet-powered airliner.

## Year - 1940
 The animated short <i>[A Wild Hare](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Wild_Hare "A Wild Hare")</i> is released, introducing the character of [Bugs Bunny](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bugs_Bunny "Bugs Bunny").

## Year - 1929
 The [Geneva Convention of 1929](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Geneva_Convention_on_Prisoners_of_War_(1929) "Geneva Convention on Prisoners of War (1929)"), dealing with treatment of prisoners-of-war, is signed by 53 nations.

## Year - 1921
 Researchers at the [University of Toronto](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Toronto "University of Toronto"), led by [biochemist](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Biochemist "Biochemist") [Frederick Banting](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Frederick_Banting "Frederick Banting"), prove that the [hormone](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hormone "Hormone") [insulin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Insulin "Insulin") regulates [blood sugar](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Blood_sugar "Blood sugar").

## Year - 1919
 The [Chicago Race Riot](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chicago_race_riot_of_1919 "Chicago race riot of 1919") erupts after a racial incident occurred on a [South Side](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Side,_Chicago "South Side, Chicago") beach, leading to 38 fatalities and 537 injuries over a five-day period.

## Year - 1917
 [World War I](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_I "World War I"): The [Allies](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_I "Allies of World War I") reach the [Yser Canal](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Yser "Yser") at the [Battle of Passchendaele](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Passchendaele "Battle of Passchendaele").

## Year - 1890
 [Vincent van Gogh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vincent_van_Gogh "Vincent van Gogh") shoots himself and dies two days later.

## Year - 1880
 [Second Anglo-Afghan War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Anglo-Afghan_War "Second Anglo-Afghan War"): [Battle of Maiwand](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Maiwand "Battle of Maiwand"): [Afghan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan") forces led by [Mohammad Ayub Khan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mohammad_Ayub_Khan_(Emir_of_Afghanistan) "Mohammad Ayub Khan (Emir of Afghanistan)") defeat the British Army in battle near [Maiwand](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Maiwand "Maiwand"), [Afghanistan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan").

## Year - 1866
 The first permanent [transatlantic telegraph cable](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Transatlantic_telegraph_cable "Transatlantic telegraph cable") is successfully completed, stretching from [Valentia Island](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Valentia_Island "Valentia Island"), Ireland, to [Heart's Content](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Heart%27s_Content,_Newfoundland_and_Labrador "Heart's Content, Newfoundland and Labrador"), [Newfoundland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Newfoundland_(island) "Newfoundland (island)").

## Year - 1865
 [Welsh settlers](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Y_Wladfa "Y Wladfa") arrive at [Chubut](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chubut_River "Chubut River") in [Argentina](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Argentina "Argentina").

## Year - 1857
 Indian Rebellion: Sixty-eight men [hold out for eight days](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Arrah "Siege of Arrah") against a force of 2,500 to 3,000 mutinying sepoys and 8,000 irregular forces.

## Year - 1816
 Seminole Wars: The [Battle of Negro Fort](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Negro_Fort "Battle of Negro Fort") ends when a hot shot cannonball fired by US Navy Gunboat No. 154 explodes the fort's Powder Magazine, killing approximately 275. It is considered the deadliest single cannon shot in US history.

## Year - 1794
 [French Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Revolution "French Revolution"): [Maximilien Robespierre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Maximilien_Robespierre "Maximilien Robespierre") is [arrested](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Thermidorian_Reaction "Thermidorian Reaction") after encouraging the execution of more than 17,000 "enemies of the Revolution".

## Year - 1789
 The first U.S. [federal government](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_government_of_the_United_States "Federal government of the United States") agency, the [Department of Foreign Affairs](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Department_of_State#History "United States Department of State"), is established (it will be later renamed [Department of State](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Department_of_State "United States Department of State")).

## Year - 1778
 [American Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Revolution "American Revolution"): [First Battle of Ushant](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Ushant_(1778) "Battle of Ushant (1778)"): [British](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Great_Britain "Kingdom of Great Britain") and French fleets fight to a standoff.

## Year - 1775
 Founding of the [U.S. Army Medical Department](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Army_Medical_Department_(United_States) "Army Medical Department (United States)"): The [Second Continental Congress](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Continental_Congress "Second Continental Congress") passes legislation establishing "an hospital for [an army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Continental_Army "Continental Army") consisting of 20,000 men."

## Year - 1714
 The [Great Northern War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Northern_War "Great Northern War"): The first significant victory of the [Russian Navy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Navy "Russian Navy") in the [naval battle of Gangut](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Gangut "Battle of Gangut") against the [Swedish Navy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Swedish_Navy "Swedish Navy") near the [Hanko Peninsula](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hanko_Peninsula "Hanko Peninsula").

## Year - 1694
 A [Royal charter](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_charter "Royal charter") is granted to the [Bank of England](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bank_of_England "Bank of England").

## Year - 1689
 [Glorious Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Glorious_Revolution "Glorious Revolution"): The [Battle of Killiecrankie](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Killiecrankie "Battle of Killiecrankie") is a victory for the Jacobites.

## Year - 1663
 The [English Parliament](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Parliament_of_England "Parliament of England") passes the [second Navigation Act](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Navigation_Acts#The_Navigation_Acts "Navigation Acts") requiring that all goods bound for the [American colonies](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Thirteen_Colonies "Thirteen Colonies") have to be sent in English ships from English ports. After the [Acts of Union 1707](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Acts_of_Union_1707 "Acts of Union 1707"), Scotland would be included in the Act.

## Year - 1549
 The [Jesuit](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Society_of_Jesus "Society of Jesus") priest [Francis Xavier](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_Xavier "Francis Xavier")'s ship reaches Japan.

## Year - 1302
 [Battle of Bapheus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Bapheus "Battle of Bapheus"): Decisive [Ottoman](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ottoman_Empire "Ottoman Empire") victory over the [Byzantines](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Empire "Byzantine Empire") opening up [Bithynia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bithynia "Bithynia") for Turkish conquest.

## Year - 1299
 According to [Edward Gibbon](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_Gibbon "Edward Gibbon"), [Osman I](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Osman_I "Osman I") invades the territory of [Nicomedia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicomedia "Nicomedia") for the first time, usually considered to be the founding day of the [Ottoman state](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ottoman_state "Ottoman state").

## Year - 1214
 [Battle of Bouvines](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Bouvines "Battle of Bouvines"): [Philip II of France](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Philip_II_of_France "Philip II of France") decisively defeats [Imperial](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Holy_Roman_Empire "Holy Roman Empire"), [English](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_England "Kingdom of England") and [Flemish](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/County_of_Flanders "County of Flanders") armies, effectively ending [John of England](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/John,_King_of_England "John, King of England")'s [Angevin Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Angevin_Empire "Angevin Empire").

## Year - 1202
 [Georgian-Seljuk wars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Georgian%E2%80%93Seljuk_wars "Georgian-Seljuk wars"): At the [Battle of Basian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Basian "Battle of Basian") the [Kingdom of Georgia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Georgia "Kingdom of Georgia") defeats the [Sultanate of Rum](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sultanate_of_Rum "Sultanate of Rum").
