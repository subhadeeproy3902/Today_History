
# Today's History 

5th August 2025


## Year - 2024
 Following the [non-cooperation movement](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Non-cooperation_movement_(2024) "Non-cooperation movement (2024)") against the [government of Bangladesh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Fifth_Hasina_ministry "Fifth Hasina ministry"), Bangladeshi prime minister [Sheikh Hasina resigns and flees the country](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Resignation_of_Sheikh_Hasina "Resignation of Sheikh Hasina"), ending her rule of 15 consecutive years and a total of almost two decades.

## Year - 2021
 [Australia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Australia "Australia")'s second most populous state [Victoria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Victoria_(state) "Victoria (state)") enters its sixth [COVID-19](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/COVID-19 "COVID-19") [lockdown](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lockdown "Lockdown"), enacting stage four restrictions statewide in reaction to six new COVID-19 cases recorded that morning.

## Year - 2019
 The [revocation of the special status](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Revocation_of_the_special_status_of_Jammu_and_Kashmir "Revocation of the special status of Jammu and Kashmir") of [Jammu and Kashmir (state)](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jammu_and_Kashmir_(state) "Jammu and Kashmir (state)") occurred and the state was bifurcated into two union territories ([Jammu and Kashmir (union territory)](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jammu_and_Kashmir_(union_territory) "Jammu and Kashmir (union territory)") and [Ladakh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ladakh "Ladakh")).

## Year - 2015
 The Environmental Protection Agency at [Gold King Mine waste water spill](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Gold_King_Mine_waste_water_spill "2015 Gold King Mine waste water spill") releases three million gallons of [heavy metal toxin tailings](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Toxic_heavy_metal "Toxic heavy metal") and waste water into the [Animas River](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Animas_River "Animas River") in Colorado.

## Year - 2012
 The [Wisconsin Sikh temple shooting](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wisconsin_Sikh_temple_shooting "Wisconsin Sikh temple shooting") took place in [Oak Creek, Wisconsin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Oak_Creek,_Wisconsin "Oak Creek, Wisconsin"), killing six victims; the perpetrator committed suicide after being wounded by police.

## Year - 2010
 Ten members of [International Assistance Mission](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Assistance_Mission "International Assistance Mission") Nuristan Eye Camp team [are killed](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2010_Badakhshan_massacre "2010 Badakhshan massacre") by persons unknown in [Kuran wa Munjan District](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuran_wa_Munjan_District "Kuran wa Munjan District") of [Badakhshan Province, Afghanistan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Badakhshan_Province "Badakhshan Province").

## Year - 2008
 The [New England Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/New_England_Revolution "New England Revolution") win the [2008 North American SuperLiga final](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2008_North_American_SuperLiga_final "2008 North American SuperLiga final") against the [Houston Dynamo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Houston_Dynamo "Houston Dynamo").

## Year - 2003
 A [car bomb](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2003_Marriott_Hotel_bombing "2003 Marriott Hotel bombing") explodes in the [Indonesian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesia "Indonesia") capital of [Jakarta](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jakarta "Jakarta") outside the [Marriott Hotel](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Marriott_International "Marriott International") killing 12 and injuring 150.

## Year - 1995
 [Yugoslav Wars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Yugoslav_Wars "Yugoslav Wars"): The city of [Knin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Knin "Knin"), Croatia, a significant [Serb](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Serbs "Serbs") stronghold, is taken by [Croatian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Croatia "Croatia") forces during [Operation Storm](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Storm "Operation Storm"). The date is celebrated in Croatia as [Victory Day](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Victory_Day_(Croatia) "Victory Day (Croatia)").

## Year - 1984
 A [Biman Bangladesh Airlines](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Biman_Bangladesh_Airlines "Biman Bangladesh Airlines") [Fokker F27 Friendship](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Fokker_F27_Friendship "Fokker F27 Friendship") [crashes](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1984_Biman_Bangladesh_Airlines_Fokker_F27_crash "1984 Biman Bangladesh Airlines Fokker F27 crash") on approach to [Zia International Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hazrat_Shahjalal_International_Airport "Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport"), in [Dhaka](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dhaka "Dhaka"), [Bangladesh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bangladesh "Bangladesh"), killing all 49 people on board.

## Year - 1981
 President [Ronald Reagan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ronald_Reagan "Ronald Reagan") [fires](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Professional_Air_Traffic_Controllers_Organization_(1968) "Professional Air Traffic Controllers Organization (1968)") 11,359 [striking](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Strike_action "Strike action") air-traffic controllers who ignored his order for them to return to work.

## Year - 1979
 In Afghanistan, Maoists undertake the [Bala Hissar uprising](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bala_Hissar_uprising "Bala Hissar uprising") against the Leninist government.

## Year - 1974
 [Watergate scandal](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Watergate_scandal "Watergate scandal"): President [Richard Nixon](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Nixon "Richard Nixon"), under orders of the [US Supreme Court](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_the_United_States "Supreme Court of the United States"), releases the ["Smoking Gun" tape](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nixon_White_House_tapes "Nixon White House tapes"), recorded on June 23, 1972, clearly revealing his actions in covering up and interfering investigations into the break-in. His political support vanishes completely.

## Year - 1973
 [Mars 6](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mars_6 "Mars 6") is launched from the USSR.

## Year - 1971
 The first [Pacific Islands Forum](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Islands_Forum "Pacific Islands Forum") (then known as the "South Pacific Forum") is held in [Wellington](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wellington "Wellington"), New Zealand, with the aim of enhancing cooperation between the independent countries of the Pacific Ocean.

## Year - 1969
 The [<i>Lonesome Cowboys</i> police raid](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lonesome_Cowboys_police_raid "Lonesome Cowboys police raid") occurs in [Atlanta, Georgia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlanta,_Georgia "Atlanta, Georgia"), leading to the creation of the Georgia Gay Liberation Front.

## Year - 1966
 A group of [red guards](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Guards "Red Guards") at [Experimental High](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Experimental_High_School_Attached_to_Beijing_Normal_University "Experimental High School Attached to Beijing Normal University") in Beijing, including [Deng Rong](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Deng_Rong "Deng Rong") and Liu Pingping, daughters of [Deng Xiaoping](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Deng_Xiaoping "Deng Xiaoping") and [Liu Shaoqi](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Liu_Shaoqi "Liu Shaoqi") respectively, beat the deputy vice principal, Bian Zhongyun, to death with sticks after accusing her of counter-revolutionary revisionism, producing one of the first fatalities of the [Cultural Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cultural_Revolution "Cultural Revolution").

## Year - 1965
 The [Indo-Pakistani war of 1965](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Indo-Pakistani_war_of_1965 "Indo-Pakistani war of 1965") begins as Pakistani soldiers cross the [Line of Control](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Line_of_Control "Line of Control") dressed as locals.

## Year - 1964
 [Vietnam War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam_War "Vietnam War"): [Operation Pierce Arrow](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Pierce_Arrow "Operation Pierce Arrow"): American aircraft from carriers [USS<i>Ticonderoga</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Ticonderoga_(CV-14) "USS Ticonderoga (CV-14)") and [USS<i>Constellation</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Constellation_(CV-64) "USS Constellation (CV-64)") bomb [North Vietnam](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Vietnam "North Vietnam") in retaliation for strikes against U.S. destroyers in the [Gulf of Tonkin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulf_of_Tonkin "Gulf of Tonkin").

## Year - 1963
 [Cold War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cold_War "Cold War"): The United States, the United Kingdom, and the Soviet Union sign the [Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Partial_Nuclear_Test_Ban_Treaty "Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty").

## Year - 1962
 American actress [Marilyn Monroe](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Marilyn_Monroe "Marilyn Monroe") is [found dead](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Death_of_Marilyn_Monroe "Death of Marilyn Monroe") at her home from a drug overdose.

## Year - 1962
 [Apartheid](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Apartheid "Apartheid"): [Nelson Mandela](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nelson_Mandela "Nelson Mandela") is jailed. He would not be released until [1990](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1990 "1990").

## Year - 1960
 [Burkina Faso](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Burkina_Faso "Burkina Faso"), then known as [Upper Volta](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Upper_Volta "Republic of Upper Volta"), becomes independent from France.

## Year - 1957
 <i>[American Bandstand](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Bandstand "American Bandstand")</i>, a show dedicated to the teenage "baby-boomers" by playing the songs and showing popular dances of the time, debuts on the [ABC](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Broadcasting_Company "American Broadcasting Company") television network.

## Year - 1949
 In [Montana](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Montana "Montana"), 12 [smokejumper](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Smokejumper "Smokejumper") firefighters and 1 [US Forest Service](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Forest_Service "United States Forest Service") fire guard are killed in the [Mann Gulch Fire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mann_Gulch_fire "Mann Gulch fire").

## Year - 1949
 In [Ecuador](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ecuador "Ecuador"), [an earthquake](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1949_Ambato_earthquake "1949 Ambato earthquake") destroys 50 towns and kills more than 6,000.

## Year - 1944
 World War II: The Nazis begin a [week-long massacre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wola_massacre "Wola massacre") of between 40,000 and 50,000 civilians and prisoners of war in [Wola](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wola "Wola"), Poland.

## Year - 1944
 World War II: Polish insurgents liberate a German [labor camp](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Labor_camp "Labor camp") ([Gsi贸wka](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/G%C4%99si%C3%B3wka "Gsi贸wka")) in [Warsaw](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Warsaw "Warsaw"), freeing 348 [Jewish](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jews "Jews") prisoners.

## Year - 1944
 World War II: At least 1,104 [Japanese](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_people "Japanese people") [POWs in Australia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prisoner_of_war "Prisoner of war") [attempt to escape from a camp at Cowra, New South Wales](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cowra_breakout "Cowra breakout"); 545 temporarily succeed but are later either killed, commit suicide, or are recaptured.

## Year - 1940
 [World War II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II "World War II"): The [Soviet Union](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union") formally [annexes](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_occupation_of_Latvia_in_1940#1940-1941:_The_first_Soviet_occupation "Soviet occupation of Latvia in 1940") [Latvia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Latvia "Latvia").

## Year - 1939
 [The Thirteen Roses](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Thirteen_Roses "The Thirteen Roses"): Thirteen female members of the [Unified Socialist Youth](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Unified_Socialist_Youth "Unified Socialist Youth") are executed by [Francoist](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Francoist "Francoist") forces in [Madrid](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Madrid "Madrid"), Spain.

## Year - 1926
 [Harry Houdini](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Harry_Houdini "Harry Houdini") performs his greatest feat, spending 91 minutes underwater in a sealed tank before escaping.

## Year - 1925
 [Plaid Cymru](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Plaid_Cymru "Plaid Cymru") is formed with the aim of disseminating knowledge of the [Welsh language](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Welsh_language "Welsh language") that is at the time in danger of dying out.

## Year - 1916
 World War I: [Battle of Romani](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Romani "Battle of Romani"): Allied forces, under the command of [Archibald Murray](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Archibald_Murray "Archibald Murray"), defeat an attacking [Ottoman army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ottoman_Empire "Ottoman Empire") under the command of [Friedrich Freiherr Kress von Kressenstein](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Friedrich_Freiherr_Kress_von_Kressenstein "Friedrich Freiherr Kress von Kressenstein"), securing the [Suez Canal](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Suez_Canal "Suez Canal") and beginning the Ottoman retreat from the [Sinai Peninsula](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sinai_Peninsula "Sinai Peninsula").

## Year - 1914
 In [Cleveland, Ohio](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cleveland,_Ohio "Cleveland, Ohio"), the first electric [traffic light](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Traffic_light "Traffic light") is installed.

## Year - 1914
 World War I: The guns of Point Nepean fort at Port Phillip Heads in Victoria (Australia) fire across the bows of the Norddeutscher Lloyd steamer [SS<i>Pfalz</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/SS_Pfalz_(1913) "SS Pfalz (1913)") which is attempting to leave the Port of Melbourne in ignorance of the declaration of war and she is detained; this is said to be the first Allied shot of the War.

## Year - 1914
 [World War I](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_I "World War I"): The German [minelayer](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Minelayer "Minelayer") [SS<i>K枚nigin Luise</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/SS_K%C3%B6nigin_Luise_(1913) "SS K枚nigin Luise (1913)") lays a minefield about 40 miles (64km) off the [Thames Estuary](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Thames_Estuary "Thames Estuary") ([Lowestoft](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lowestoft "Lowestoft")). She is intercepted and sunk by the British light-cruiser [HMS<i>Amphion</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Amphion_(1911) "HMS Amphion (1911)").

## Year - 1906
 [Persian Constitutional Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Persian_Constitutional_Revolution "Persian Constitutional Revolution"): [Mozaffar ad-Din Shah Qajar](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mozaffar_ad-Din_Shah_Qajar "Mozaffar ad-Din Shah Qajar"), King of Iran, agrees to convert the government to a [constitutional monarchy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitutional_monarchy "Constitutional monarchy").

## Year - 1901
 [Peter O'Connor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_O%27Connor_(athlete) "Peter O'Connor (athlete)") sets the first [World Athletics](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Athletics "World Athletics") recognised [long jump](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Long_jump "Long jump") [world record](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_world_records_in_athletics "List of world records in athletics") of 24ft 11.75in (7.6137m), a record that would stand for 20 years.

## Year - 1888
 [Bertha Benz](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bertha_Benz "Bertha Benz") drives from [Mannheim](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mannheim "Mannheim") to [Pforzheim](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pforzheim "Pforzheim") and back in the first long distance automobile trip, commemorated as the [Bertha Benz Memorial Route](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bertha_Benz_Memorial_Route "Bertha Benz Memorial Route") since 2008.

## Year - 1884
 The cornerstone for the [Statue of Liberty](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Statue_of_Liberty "Statue of Liberty") is laid on [Bedloe's Island](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Liberty_Island "Liberty Island") (now Liberty Island) in [New York Harbor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_Harbor "New York Harbor").

## Year - 1882
 [Standard Oil](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Standard_Oil "Standard Oil") Company of New Jersey, today known as [ExxonMobil](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/ExxonMobil "ExxonMobil"), is established officially. The company would later grow to become the holder of all Standard Oil companies and the entity at the center of the [breakup of Standard Oil](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Breakup_of_Standard_Oil "Breakup of Standard Oil").

## Year - 1874
 Japan launches its [postal savings system](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Postal_savings_system "Postal savings system"), modeled after a similar system in the United Kingdom.

## Year - 1864
 American Civil War: The [Battle of Mobile Bay](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Mobile_Bay "Battle of Mobile Bay") begins at [Mobile Bay](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mobile_Bay "Mobile Bay") near [Mobile, Alabama](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mobile,_Alabama "Mobile, Alabama"), Admiral [David Farragut](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Farragut "David Farragut") leads a Union flotilla through Confederate defenses and seals one of the last major Southern ports.

## Year - 1862
 American Civil War: [Battle of Baton Rouge](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Baton_Rouge_(1862) "Battle of Baton Rouge (1862)"): Along the [Mississippi River](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi_River "Mississippi River") near [Baton Rouge, Louisiana](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Baton_Rouge,_Louisiana "Baton Rouge, Louisiana"), [Confederate](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America "Confederate States of America") troops attempt to take the city, but are driven back by fire from Union gunboats.

## Year - 1861
 The [United States Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Army "United States Army") abolishes [flogging](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Flagellation "Flagellation").

## Year - 1861
 [American Civil War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Civil_War "American Civil War"): In order to help pay for the war effort, the [United States government](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_government_of_the_United_States "Federal government of the United States") levies the first [income tax](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Income_tax "Income tax") as part of the [Revenue Act of 1861](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Revenue_Act_of_1861 "Revenue Act of 1861") (3% of all incomes over US$800; rescinded in [1872](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1872 "1872")).

## Year - 1860
 [Charles XV](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_XV "Charles XV") of Sweden-Norway is crowned king of Norway in [Trondheim](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Trondheim "Trondheim").

## Year - 1858
 [Cyrus West Field](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyrus_West_Field "Cyrus West Field") and others complete the first [transatlantic telegraph cable](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Transatlantic_telegraph_cable "Transatlantic telegraph cable") after several unsuccessful attempts. It will operate for less than a month.

## Year - 1824
 [Greek War of Independence](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Greek_War_of_Independence "Greek War of Independence"): [Konstantinos Kanaris](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Konstantinos_Kanaris "Konstantinos Kanaris") leads a Greek fleet to victory against Ottoman and Egyptian naval forces in the [Battle of Samos](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Samos_(1824) "Battle of Samos (1824)").

## Year - 1816
 The British Admiralty dismisses [Francis Ronalds](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_Ronalds "Francis Ronalds")'s new invention of the first working [electric telegraph](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Electrical_telegraph "Electrical telegraph") as "wholly unnecessary", preferring to continue using the semaphore.

## Year - 1796
 The [Battle of Castiglione](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Castiglione "Battle of Castiglione") in [Napoleon](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Napoleon "Napoleon")'s first [Italian campaigns of the French Revolutionary Wars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Italian_campaigns_of_the_French_Revolutionary_Wars "Italian campaigns of the French Revolutionary Wars") results in a French victory.

## Year - 1781
 The [Battle of Dogger Bank](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Dogger_Bank_(1781) "Battle of Dogger Bank (1781)") takes place.

## Year - 1772
 [First Partition of Poland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Partition_of_Poland "First Partition of Poland"): The representatives of [Austria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Habsburg_monarchy "Habsburg monarchy"), [Prussia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prussia "Prussia"), and [Russia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Empire "Russian Empire") sign three bilateral conventions condemning the "anarchy" of the [Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Polish%E2%80%93Lithuanian_Commonwealth "Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth") and imputing to the three powers "ancient and legitimate rights" to the territories of the Commonwealth. The conventions allow each of the three great powers to annex a part of the Commonwealth, which they proceed to do over the course of the following two months.

## Year - 1763
 [Pontiac's War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pontiac%27s_War "Pontiac's War"): [Battle of Bushy Run](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Bushy_Run "Battle of Bushy Run"): British forces led by [Henry Bouquet](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_Bouquet "Henry Bouquet") defeat [Chief Pontiac](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chief_Pontiac "Chief Pontiac")'s [Indians](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Native_Americans_in_the_United_States "Native Americans in the United States") at [Bushy Run](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Bushy_Run "Battle of Bushy Run").

## Year - 1735
 [Freedom of the press](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Freedom_of_the_press "Freedom of the press"): <i>New York Weekly Journal</i> writer [John Peter Zenger](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Peter_Zenger "John Peter Zenger") is acquitted of [seditious libel](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Seditious_libel "Seditious libel") against the royal governor of New York, on the basis that what he had published was true.

## Year - 1716
 [Austro-Turkish War (1716-1718)](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Austro-Turkish_War_(1716%E2%80%931718) "Austro-Turkish War (1716-1718)"): One-fifth of a Turkish army and the [Grand Vizier](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grand_Vizier "Grand Vizier") are killed in the [Battle of Petrovaradin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Petrovaradin "Battle of Petrovaradin").

## Year - 1689
 [Beaver Wars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Beaver_Wars "Beaver Wars"): Fifteen hundred [Iroquois](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Iroquois "Iroquois") [attack](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lachine_massacre "Lachine massacre") [Lachine](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lachine,_Quebec "Lachine, Quebec") in [New France](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/New_France "New France").

## Year - 1620
 The <i>[Mayflower](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mayflower "Mayflower")</i> departs from [Southampton](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Southampton "Southampton"), England, carrying would-be settlers, on its first attempt to reach North America; it is forced to dock in Dartmouth when its companion ship, the <i>Speedwell</i>, springs a leak.

## Year - 1600
 The [Gowrie Conspiracy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Ruthven,_3rd_Earl_of_Gowrie#Gowrie_conspiracy "John Ruthven, 3rd Earl of Gowrie") against [King James VI of Scotland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/James_VI_and_I "James VI and I") (later to become King James I of England) takes place at [Gowrie House (Perth, Scotland)](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gowrie_House_(Perth,_Scotland) "Gowrie House (Perth, Scotland)").

## Year - 1583
 Sir [Humphrey Gilbert](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Humphrey_Gilbert "Humphrey Gilbert") establishes the first English [colony](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Colony "Colony") in North America, at what is now [St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/St._John%27s,_Newfoundland_and_Labrador "St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador").

## Year - 1506
 The [Grand Duchy of Lithuania](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Lithuania "Grand Duchy of Lithuania") defeats the [Crimean Khanate](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Crimean_Khanate "Crimean Khanate") in the [Battle of Kletsk](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Kletsk "Battle of Kletsk").

## Year - 1460
 The Kingdom of Scotland [captures Roxburgh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Capture_of_Roxburgh_(1460) "Capture of Roxburgh (1460)"), one of the last English strongholds in Scotland, following a siege.

## Year - 1388
 The [Battle of Otterburn](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Otterburn "Battle of Otterburn"), a border skirmish between the Scottish and the English in Northern England, is fought near [Otterburn](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Otterburn,_Northumberland "Otterburn, Northumberland").

## Year - 1305
 [First Scottish War of Independence](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Scottish_War_of_Independence "First Scottish War of Independence"): [Sir John Stewart of Menteith](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/John_de_Menteith "John de Menteith"), the pro-English Sheriff of Dumbarton, successfully manages to capture [Sir William Wallace of Scotland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Wallace "William Wallace"), leading to Wallace's subsequent execution by hanging, evisceration, drawing and quartering, and beheading 18 days later.

## Year - 1278
 [Spanish Reconquista](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish_Reconquista "Spanish Reconquista"): the forces of the [Kingdom of Castile](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Castile "Kingdom of Castile") initiate the ultimately futile [Siege of Algeciras](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Algeciras_(1278) "Siege of Algeciras (1278)") against the [Emirate of Granada](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Emirate_of_Granada "Emirate of Granada").

## Year - 1100
 [Henry I](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_I_of_England "Henry I of England") is crowned [King of England](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_English_monarchs "List of English monarchs") in [Westminster Abbey](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Westminster_Abbey "Westminster Abbey").

## Year - 1068
