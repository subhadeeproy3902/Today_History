
# Today's History 📜

26th June 2024


## Year - 2015
 The [U.S. Supreme Court](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_the_United_States "Supreme Court of the United States") [ruled, 5-4](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Obergefell_v._Hodges "Obergefell v. Hodges"), that [same-sex couples](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Same-sex_relationship "Same-sex relationship") have a [constitutional right](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitutional_right "Constitutional right") to [marriage](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Marriage "Marriage") under the [14th Amendment to the United States Constitution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Fourteenth_Amendment_to_the_United_States_Constitution "Fourteenth Amendment to the United States Constitution").

## Year - 2015
 Five different terrorist attacks in France, Tunisia, Somalia, Kuwait, and Syria occurred on what was dubbed [Bloody Friday](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/26_June_2015_Islamist_attacks "26 June 2015 Islamist attacks") by international media. Upwards of 750 people were either killed or injured in these uncoordinated attacks.

## Year - 2013
 The [U.S. Supreme Court](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_the_United_States "Supreme Court of the United States") [ruled, 5-4](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_v._Windsor "United States v. Windsor"), that Section 3 of the [Defense of Marriage Act](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Defense_of_Marriage_Act "Defense of Marriage Act") is unconstitutional and in violation of the [Fifth Amendment to the United States Constitution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Fifth_Amendment_to_the_United_States_Constitution "Fifth Amendment to the United States Constitution").

## Year - 2013
 [Riots](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/June_2013_Shanshan_riots "June 2013 Shanshan riots") in China's [Xinjiang](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Xinjiang "Xinjiang") region kill at least 36 people and injure 21 others.

## Year - 2012
 The [Waldo Canyon fire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Waldo_Canyon_fire "Waldo Canyon fire") descends into the Mountain Shadows neighborhood in [Colorado Springs](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Colorado_Springs,_Colorado "Colorado Springs, Colorado") burning 347 homes in a matter of hours and killing two people.

## Year - 2008
 A suicide bomber dressed as an Iraqi policeman detonates an explosive vest, [killing 25 people](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2008_Karmah_Bombing "2008 Karmah Bombing").

## Year - 2007
 [Pope Benedict XVI](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Benedict_XVI "Pope Benedict XVI") reinstates the traditional laws of papal election in which a successful candidate must receive two-thirds of the votes.

## Year - 2006
 [Mari Alkatiri](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mari_Alkatiri "Mari Alkatiri"), the first [Prime Minister of East Timor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_East_Timor "Prime Minister of East Timor"), resigns after weeks of political unrest.

## Year - 2003
 The [U.S. Supreme Court](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_the_United_States "Supreme Court of the United States") rules in <i>[Lawrence v. Texas](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lawrence_v._Texas "Lawrence v. Texas")</i> that sex-based [sodomy laws](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sodomy_law "Sodomy law") are unconstitutional.

## Year - 2000
 The [Human Genome Project](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Human_Genome_Project "Human Genome Project") announces the completion of a "rough draft" sequence.

## Year - 1997
 [J. K. Rowling](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/J._K._Rowling "J. K. Rowling") publishes the first of her <i>[Harry Potter](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Harry_Potter "Harry Potter")</i> novel series, <i>[Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Philosopher%27s_Stone "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone")</i> in United Kingdom.

## Year - 1997
 The [U.S. Supreme Court](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_the_United_States "Supreme Court of the United States") rules that the [Communications Decency Act](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Communications_Decency_Act "Communications Decency Act") violates the [First Amendment to the United States Constitution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Amendment_to_the_United_States_Constitution "First Amendment to the United States Constitution").

## Year - 1995
 [Hamad bin Khalifa Al Thani](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamad_bin_Khalifa_Al_Thani "Hamad bin Khalifa Al Thani") deposes his father [Khalifa bin Hamad Al Thani](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Khalifa_bin_Hamad_Al_Thani "Khalifa bin Hamad Al Thani"), the [Emir](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Emir "Emir") of [Qatar](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Qatar "Qatar"), in a bloodless [coup d'état](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Coup_d%27%C3%A9tat "Coup d'").

## Year - 1991
 [Yugoslav Wars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Yugoslav_Wars "Yugoslav Wars"): The [Yugoslav People's Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Yugoslav_People%27s_Army "Yugoslav People's Army") begins the [Ten-Day War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ten-Day_War "Ten-Day War") in [Slovenia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Slovenia "Slovenia").

## Year - 1988
 The first crash of an [Airbus A320](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Airbus_A320_family "Airbus A320 family") occurs when [Air France Flight 296Q](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Air_France_Flight_296Q "Air France Flight 296Q") crashes at [Mulhouse-Habsheim Airfield](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mulhouse%E2%80%93Habsheim_Airfield "Mulhouse-Habsheim Airfield") in [Habsheim](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Habsheim "Habsheim"), France, during an [air show](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Air_show "Air show"), killing three of the 136 people on board.

## Year - 1981
 [Dan-Air Flight 240](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dan-Air_Flight_240 "Dan-Air Flight 240"), flying to [East Midlands Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/East_Midlands_Airport "East Midlands Airport"), crashes in [Nailstone](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nailstone "Nailstone"), [Leicestershire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Leicestershire "Leicestershire"). All three crew members perish.

## Year - 1978
 [Air Canada Flight 189](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Air_Canada_Flight_189 "Air Canada Flight 189"), flying to Toronto, overruns the runway and crashes into the [Etobicoke Creek](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Etobicoke_Creek "Etobicoke Creek") [ravine](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ravine "Ravine"). Two of the 107 passengers on board perish.

## Year - 1977
 [Elvis Presley](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Elvis_Presley "Elvis Presley") held his final concert in Indianapolis, Indiana at Market Square Arena.

## Year - 1975
 Two [FBI](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/FBI "FBI") agents and a member of the [American Indian Movement](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Indian_Movement "American Indian Movement") are killed in a shootout on the [Pine Ridge Indian Reservation](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pine_Ridge_Indian_Reservation "Pine Ridge Indian Reservation") in [South Dakota](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Dakota "South Dakota"); [Leonard Peltier](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Leonard_Peltier "Leonard Peltier") is later convicted of the murders in a controversial trial.

## Year - 1974
 The [Universal Product Code](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Universal_Product_Code "Universal Product Code") is scanned for the first time to sell a package of Wrigley's [chewing gum](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chewing_gum "Chewing gum") at the Marsh Supermarket in [Troy, Ohio](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Troy,_Ohio "Troy, Ohio").

## Year - 1967
 [Karol Wojtyła](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Karol_Wojty%C5%82a "Karol Wojty") (later John Paul II) made a cardinal by [Pope Paul VI](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Paul_VI "Pope Paul VI").

## Year - 1963
 Cold War: U.S. President [John F. Kennedy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/John_F._Kennedy "John F. Kennedy") gave his "[Ich bin ein Berliner](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ich_bin_ein_Berliner "Ich bin ein Berliner")" speech, underlining the support of the United States for democratic [West Germany](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Germany "West Germany") shortly after [Soviet](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union")-supported [East Germany](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/East_Germany "East Germany") erected the [Berlin Wall](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Berlin_Wall "Berlin Wall").

## Year - 1960
 [Madagascar](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Madagascar "Madagascar") gains its independence from France.

## Year - 1960
 The former British Protectorate of [British Somaliland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Somaliland "British Somaliland") gains its independence as [Somaliland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/State_of_Somaliland "State of Somaliland").

## Year - 1959
 Swedish boxer [Ingemar Johansson](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ingemar_Johansson "Ingemar Johansson") becomes world champion of heavy weight boxing, by defeating American [Floyd Patterson](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Floyd_Patterson "Floyd Patterson") on technical knockout after two minutes and three seconds in the third round at [Yankee Stadium](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Yankee_Stadium "Yankee Stadium").

## Year - 1955
 The South African Congress Alliance adopts the [Freedom Charter](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Freedom_Charter "Freedom Charter") at the [Congress of the People](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Congress_of_the_People_(1955) "Congress of the People (1955)") in [Kliptown](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kliptown "Kliptown").

## Year - 1953
 [Lavrentiy Beria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lavrentiy_Beria "Lavrentiy Beria"), head of [MVD](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/MVD "MVD"), is arrested by [Nikita Khrushchev](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nikita_Khrushchev "Nikita Khrushchev") and other members of the [Politburo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Politburo "Politburo").

## Year - 1952
 The [Pan-Malayan Labour Party](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pan-Malayan_Labour_Party "Pan-Malayan Labour Party") is founded in [Malaya](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Federation_of_Malaya "Federation of Malaya"), as a union of statewide labour parties.

## Year - 1948
 [Shirley Jackson](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Shirley_Jackson "Shirley Jackson")'s short story <i>[The Lottery](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Lottery "The Lottery")</i> is published in <i>[The New Yorker](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_New_Yorker "The New Yorker")</i> magazine.

## Year - 1948
 [William Shockley](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Shockley "William Shockley") files the original patent for the [grown-junction transistor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grown-junction_transistor "Grown-junction transistor"), the first [bipolar junction transistor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bipolar_junction_transistor "Bipolar junction transistor").

## Year - 1948
 [Cold War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cold_War "Cold War"): The first supply flights are made in response to the [Berlin Blockade](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Berlin_Blockade "Berlin Blockade").

## Year - 1945
 The [United Nations Charter](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Charter "United Nations Charter") is signed by [50 Allied nations](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_II "Allies of World War II") in [San Francisco, California](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Francisco "San Francisco").

## Year - 1944
 World War II: The [Battle of Osuchy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Osuchy "Battle of Osuchy") in [Osuchy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Osuchy "Osuchy"), Poland, one of the largest battles between Nazi Germany and [Polish resistance forces](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Polish_resistance_movement_in_World_War_II "Polish resistance movement in World War II"), ends with the defeat of the latter.

## Year - 1944
 World War II: [San Marino](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Marino "San Marino"), a neutral state, is mistakenly bombed by the [RAF](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/RAF "RAF") based on faulty information, leading to 35 civilian deaths.

## Year - 1942
 The first flight of the [Grumman F6F Hellcat](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grumman_F6F_Hellcat "Grumman F6F Hellcat").

## Year - 1941
 World War II: Soviet planes [bomb Kassa, Hungary](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bombing_of_Kassa "Bombing of Kassa") (now [Košice](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ko%C5%A1ice "Ko"), [Slovakia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Slovakia "Slovakia")), giving Hungary the impetus to declare war the next day.

## Year - 1940
 [World War II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II "World War II"): Under the [Molotov-Ribbentrop Pact](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Molotov%E2%80%93Ribbentrop_Pact "Molotov-Ribbentrop Pact"), the [Soviet Union](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union") presents an [ultimatum](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_occupation_of_Bessarabia_and_Northern_Bukovina#Soviet_ultimatum "Soviet occupation of Bessarabia and Northern Bukovina") to [Romania](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Romania "Kingdom of Romania") requiring it to cede [Bessarabia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bessarabia "Bessarabia") and the northern part of [Bukovina](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bukovina "Bukovina").

## Year - 1936
 Initial flight of the [Focke-Wulf Fw 61](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Focke-Wulf_Fw_61 "Focke-Wulf Fw 61"), the first practical helicopter.

## Year - 1934
 United States President [Franklin D. Roosevelt](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Franklin_D._Roosevelt "Franklin D. Roosevelt") signs the [Federal Credit Union Act](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Credit_Union_Act "Federal Credit Union Act"), which establishes credit unions.

## Year - 1927
 [The Cyclone](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Cyclone "The Cyclone") roller coaster opens on [Coney Island](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Coney_Island "Coney Island").

## Year - 1924
 The [American occupation of the Dominican Republic](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_occupation_of_the_Dominican_Republic_(1916%E2%80%9324) "United States occupation of the Dominican Republic (1916-24)") ends after eight years.

## Year - 1918
 World War I: [Allied forces](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_I "Allies of World War I") under [John J. Pershing](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/John_J._Pershing "John J. Pershing") and [James Harbord](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Harbord "James Harbord") defeat [Imperial German forces](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/German_Empire "German Empire") under [Wilhelm, German Crown Prince](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilhelm,_German_Crown_Prince "Wilhelm, German Crown Prince") in the [Battle of Belleau Wood](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Belleau_Wood "Battle of Belleau Wood").

## Year - 1917
 [World War I](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_I "World War I"): The [American Expeditionary Forces](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Expeditionary_Forces "American Expeditionary Forces") begin to arrive in France. They will first enter combat in the Battle of Hamel on the 4th of July.

## Year - 1909
 The [Science Museum](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Science_Museum,_London "Science Museum, London") in London comes into existence as an independent entity.

## Year - 1906
 The first [Grand Prix](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1906_French_Grand_Prix "1906 French Grand Prix") motor race is held at [Le Mans](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Le_Mans "Le Mans").

## Year - 1889
 [Bangui](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bangui "Bangui") is founded by Albert Dolisie and Alfred Uzac in what was then the upper reaches of the [French Congo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Congo "French Congo").

## Year - 1886
 [Henri Moissan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Henri_Moissan "Henri Moissan") isolated elemental [Fluorine](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Fluorine "Fluorine") for the first time.

## Year - 1857
 The first investiture of the [Victoria Cross](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Victoria_Cross "Victoria Cross") in [Hyde Park](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyde_Park,_London "Hyde Park, London"), London.

## Year - 1848
 End of the [June Days Uprising](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/June_Days_Uprising "June Days Uprising") in Paris.

## Year - 1843
 [Treaty of Nanking](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Nanking "Treaty of Nanking") comes into effect, [Hong Kong Island](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong_Island "Hong Kong Island") is ceded to the British "in perpetuity".

## Year - 1830
 [William IV](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/William_IV_of_the_United_Kingdom "William IV of the United Kingdom") becomes king of Britain and Hanover.

## Year - 1794
 [French Revolutionary Wars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Revolutionary_Wars "French Revolutionary Wars"): [Battle of Fleurus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Fleurus_(1794) "Battle of Fleurus (1794)") marked the first successful military use of aircraft.

## Year - 1740
 A combined force of Spanish, free blacks and allied Indians defeat a British garrison at the [Siege of Fort Mose](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Fort_Mose "Siege of Fort Mose") near [St. Augustine](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/St._Augustine,_Florida "St. Augustine, Florida") during the [War of Jenkins' Ear](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_Jenkins%27_Ear "War of Jenkins' Ear").

## Year - 1723
 After a siege and bombardment by cannon, [Baku surrenders](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Russo-Persian_War_(1722%E2%80%931723) "Russo-Persian War (1722-1723)") to the Russians.

## Year - 1718
 [Alexei Petrovich, Tsarevich of Russia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexei_Petrovich,_Tsarevich_of_Russia "Alexei Petrovich, Tsarevich of Russia"), [Peter the Great](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_the_Great "Peter the Great")'s son, mysteriously dies after being sentenced to death by his father for plotting against him.

## Year - 1579
 [Livonian campaign of Stephen Báthory](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Livonian_campaign_of_Stephen_B%C3%A1thory "Livonian campaign of Stephen B") begins.

## Year - 1541
 [Francisco Pizarro](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Francisco_Pizarro "Francisco Pizarro") is assassinated in [Lima](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lima "Lima") by the son of his former companion and later antagonist, [Diego de Almagro](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Diego_de_Almagro "Diego de Almagro") the younger. Almagro is later caught and executed.

## Year - 1522
 Ottomans begin the second [Siege of Rhodes](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Rhodes_(1522) "Siege of Rhodes (1522)").

## Year - 1483
 [Richard III](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_III_of_England "Richard III of England") becomes King of England.

## Year - 1460
 [War of the Roses](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_the_Roses "War of the Roses"): [Richard Neville, 16th Earl of Warwick](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Neville,_16th_Earl_of_Warwick "Richard Neville, 16th Earl of Warwick"), and [Edward, Earl of March](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_IV_of_England "Edward IV of England"), land in England with a rebel army and march on London.

## Year - 1409
 [Western Schism](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Schism "Western Schism"): The [Roman Catholic Church](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Church "Roman Catholic Church") is led into a double [schism](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Schism_(religion) "Schism (religion)") as Petros Philargos is crowned [Pope Alexander V](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Alexander_V "Pope Alexander V") after the [Council of Pisa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Council_of_Pisa "Council of Pisa"), joining [Pope Gregory XII](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Gregory_XII "Pope Gregory XII") in Rome and [Pope Benedict XIII](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Antipope_Benedict_XIII "Antipope Benedict XIII") in [Avignon](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Avignon "Avignon").

## Year - 1407
 [Ulrich von Jungingen](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ulrich_von_Jungingen "Ulrich von Jungingen") becomes [Grand Master of the Teutonic Knights](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grand_Master_of_the_Teutonic_Knights "Grand Master of the Teutonic Knights").

## Year - 1295
 [Przemysł II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Przemys%C5%82_II "Przemys") crowned [king of Poland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/King_of_Poland "King of Poland"), following Ducal period. The white eagle is added to the [Polish coat of arms](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Coat_of_arms_of_Poland "Coat of arms of Poland").

## Year - 1243
 Mongols defeat the [Seljuk Turks](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Seljuk_Turks "Seljuk Turks") at the [Battle of Köse Dağ](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_K%C3%B6se_Da%C4%9F "Battle of K").

## Year - 699
[En no Ozuno](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/En_no_Ozuno "En no Ozuno"), a Japanese mystic and apothecary who will later be regarded as the founder of a folk religion [Shugendō](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Shugend%C5%8D "Shugend"), is banished to [Izu Ōshima](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Izu_%C5%8Cshima "Izu ").

## Year - 684
[Pope Benedict II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pope_Benedict_II "Pope Benedict II") is the last pope to require confirmation by the Byzantine emperor before taking office.

## Year - 363
Roman emperor [Julian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Julian_(emperor) "Julian (emperor)") is killed during the retreat from the [Sasanian Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sasanian_Empire "Sasanian Empire").

## Year - 221
[Roman emperor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_emperor "Roman emperor") [Elagabalus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Elagabalus "Elagabalus") adopts his cousin [Alexander Severus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexander_Severus "Alexander Severus") as his heir and receives the title of [Caesar](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Caesar_(title) "Caesar (title)").
