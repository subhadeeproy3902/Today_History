## Year - 2011
 While flying from [Seoul](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Seoul "Seoul"), [South Korea](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Korea "South Korea") to [Shanghai](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Shanghai "Shanghai"), [China](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/China "China"), [Asiana Airlines Flight 991](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Asiana_Airlines_Flight_991 "Asiana Airlines Flight 991") develops an in-flight fire in the cargo hold. ## Year - 2010
 [Airblue Flight 202](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Airblue_Flight_202 "Airblue Flight 202") crashes into the [Margalla Hills](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Margalla_Hills "Margalla Hills") north of [Islamabad, Pakistan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamabad "Islamabad"), killing all 152 people aboard. It is the deadliest aviation accident in Pakistan history and the first involving an [Airbus A321](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Airbus_A321 "Airbus A321").

## Year - 2005

In response to alarming [German](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazi_Germany "Nazi Germany") advances, all those who retreat or otherwise leave their positions without orders to do so are to be tried in a military court, with punishment ranging from duty in a [shtrafbat](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Shtrafbat "Shtrafbat") battalion, imprisonment in a [Gulag](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulag "Gulag"), or execution.
