
# Today's History 📜

1st August 2024


## Year - 2023
 Former US President [Donald Trump](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Trump "Donald Trump") is [indicted](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_prosecution_of_Donald_Trump_(election_obstruction_case) "Federal prosecution of Donald Trump (election obstruction case)") for his role in the [January 6 United States Capitol attack](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/January_6_United_States_Capitol_attack "January 6 United States Capitol attack"), his third indictment in 2023.

## Year - 2017
 A [suicide attack](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Suicide_attack "Suicide attack") on a mosque in Herat, [Afghanistan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan") kills 20 people.

## Year - 2008
 Eleven mountaineers from international expeditions died on K2, the second-highest mountain on Earth in [the worst single accident in the history of K2 mountaineering](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2008_K2_disaster "2008 K2 disaster").

## Year - 2008
 The [Beijing-Tianjin Intercity Railway](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Beijing%E2%80%93Tianjin_Intercity_Railway "Beijing-Tianjin Intercity Railway") begins operation as the fastest commuter rail system in the world.

## Year - 2007
 The [I-35W Mississippi River bridge](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/I-35W_Mississippi_River_bridge "I-35W Mississippi River bridge") spanning the [Mississippi River](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi_River "Mississippi River") in [Minneapolis, Minnesota](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Minneapolis "Minneapolis"), collapses during the evening [rush hour](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Rush_hour "Rush hour"), killing 13 people and injuring 145.

## Year - 2004
 A [supermarket fire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ycu%C3%A1_Bola%C3%B1os_supermarket_fire "Ycuá Bolaños supermarket fire") kills 396 people and injures 500 others in [Asunción, Paraguay](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Asunci%C3%B3n,_Paraguay "Asunción, Paraguay").

## Year - 1993
 The [Great Mississippi and Missouri Rivers Flood of 1993](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Mississippi_and_Missouri_Rivers_Flood_of_1993 "Great Mississippi and Missouri Rivers Flood of 1993") comes to a peak.

## Year - 1988
 A British soldier was killed in the [Inglis Barracks bombing](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Inglis_Barracks_bombing "Inglis Barracks bombing") in London, England.

## Year - 1984
 Commercial [peat](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Peat "Peat")-cutters discover the preserved [bog body](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bog_body "Bog body") of a man, called [Lindow Man](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lindow_Man "Lindow Man"), at [Lindow Moss](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lindow_Moss "Lindow Moss"), [Cheshire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cheshire "Cheshire"), England.

## Year - 1981
 [MTV](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/MTV "MTV") begins broadcasting in the United States and airs its first video, "[Video Killed the Radio Star](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Video_Killed_the_Radio_Star "Video Killed the Radio Star")" by [The Buggles](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Buggles "The Buggles").

## Year - 1980
 A [train crash](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Buttevant_Rail_Disaster "Buttevant Rail Disaster") kills 18 people in [County Cork](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/County_Cork "County Cork"), [Ireland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Ireland "Republic of Ireland").

## Year - 1980
 [Vigdís Finnbogadóttir](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vigd%C3%ADs_Finnbogad%C3%B3ttir "Vigdís Finnbogadóttir") is elected [President of Iceland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Iceland "President of Iceland") and becomes the world's first democratically elected female head of state.

## Year - 1976
 [Niki Lauda](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Niki_Lauda "Niki Lauda") has a severe accident that almost claims his life at the [German Grand Prix](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1976_German_Grand_Prix "1976 German Grand Prix") at [Nurburgring](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/N%C3%BCrburgring "Nürburgring").

## Year - 1974
 [Cyprus dispute](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyprus_dispute "Cyprus dispute"): The [United Nations Security Council](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Security_Council "United Nations Security Council") authorizes the [UNFICYP](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Peacekeeping_Force_in_Cyprus "United Nations Peacekeeping Force in Cyprus") to create the "[Green Line](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Buffer_Zone_in_Cyprus "United Nations Buffer Zone in Cyprus")", dividing [Cyprus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyprus "Cyprus") into two zones.

## Year - 1971
 [The Concert for Bangladesh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Concert_for_Bangladesh "The Concert for Bangladesh"), organized by former Beatle George Harrison, is held at Madison Square Garden in New York City.

## Year - 1968
 The coronation of [Hassanal Bolkiah](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hassanal_Bolkiah "Hassanal Bolkiah"), the 29th [Sultan of Brunei](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sultan_of_Brunei "Sultan of Brunei"), is held.

## Year - 1966
 Purges of intellectuals and imperialists becomes official China policy at the beginning of the [Cultural Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cultural_Revolution "Cultural Revolution").

## Year - 1966
 [Charles Whitman](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Whitman "Charles Whitman") kills 16 people at the [University of Texas at Austin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Texas_at_Austin "University of Texas at Austin") before being killed by the police.

## Year - 1965
 [Frank Herbert](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Frank_Herbert "Frank Herbert")'s novel, [<i>Dune</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dune_(novel) "Dune (novel)") was published for the first time. It was named as the world's best-selling science fiction novel in 2003.

## Year - 1964
 The former [Belgian Congo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Belgian_Congo "Belgian Congo") is renamed the [Democratic Republic of the Congo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo "Democratic Republic of the Congo").

## Year - 1961
 U.S. Defense Secretary [Robert McNamara](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_McNamara "Robert McNamara") orders the creation of the [Defense Intelligence Agency](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Defense_Intelligence_Agency "Defense Intelligence Agency") (DIA), the nation's first centralized military espionage organization.

## Year - 1960
 [Islamabad](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamabad "Islamabad") is declared the [federal capital](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_capital "Federal capital") of the [Government of Pakistan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_of_Pakistan "Government of Pakistan").

## Year - 1960
 [Dahomey](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dahomey "Dahomey") (later renamed [Benin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Benin "Benin")) declares independence from France.

## Year - 1957
 The United States and Canada form the [North American Aerospace Defense Command](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/North_American_Aerospace_Defense_Command "North American Aerospace Defense Command") (NORAD).

## Year - 1950
 [Guam](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Guam "Guam") is organized as an unincorporated territory of the United States as the President [Harry S. Truman](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Harry_S._Truman "Harry S. Truman") signs the [Guam Organic Act](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Guam_Organic_Act_of_1950 "Guam Organic Act of 1950").

## Year - 1946
 Leaders of the [Russian Liberation Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Liberation_Army "Russian Liberation Army"), a force of Russian prisoners of war that [collaborated with Nazi Germany](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Collaboration_with_Nazi_Germany_and_Fascist_Italy "Collaboration with Nazi Germany and Fascist Italy"), are executed in Moscow, [Soviet Union](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union") for treason.

## Year - 1944
 World War II: The [Warsaw Uprising](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Warsaw_Uprising "Warsaw Uprising") against the [Nazi German](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazism "Nazism") occupation breaks out in [Warsaw](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Warsaw "Warsaw"), Poland.

## Year - 1943
 [World War II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II "World War II"): [Operation Tidal Wave](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Tidal_Wave "Operation Tidal Wave") also known as "Black Sunday", was a failed American attempt to destroy Romanian oil fields.

## Year - 1937
 [Josip Broz Tito](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Josip_Broz_Tito "Josip Broz Tito") reads the resolution "Manifesto of constitutional congress of KPH" to the constitutive congress of KPH ([Croatian Communist Party](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/League_of_Communists_of_Croatia "League of Communists of Croatia")) in woods near [Samobor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Samobor "Samobor").

## Year - 1936
 The [Olympics](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1936_Summer_Olympics "1936 Summer Olympics") opened in [Berlin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Berlin "Berlin") with a ceremony presided over by [Adolf Hitler](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Adolf_Hitler "Adolf Hitler").

## Year - 1933
 Anti-Fascist activists [Bruno Tesch](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bruno_Tesch_(antifascist) "Bruno Tesch (antifascist)"), Walter Möller, Karl Wolff and August Lütgens are executed by the Nazi regime in [Altona](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Altona,_Hamburg "Altona, Hamburg").

## Year - 1927
 The [Nanchang Uprising](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nanchang_Uprising "Nanchang Uprising") marks the first significant battle in the [Chinese Civil War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_Civil_War "Chinese Civil War") between the [Kuomintang](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuomintang "Kuomintang") and [Chinese Communist Party](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_Communist_Party "Chinese Communist Party"). This day is commemorated as the anniversary of the founding of the [People's Liberation Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/People%27s_Liberation_Army "People's Liberation Army").

## Year - 1915
 [Patrick Pearse](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Patrick_Pearse "Patrick Pearse") gives his famous speech "[Ireland unfree shall never be at peace](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ireland_unfree_shall_never_be_at_peace "Ireland unfree shall never be at peace")" at [O'Donovan Rossa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/O%27Donovan_Rossa "O'Donovan Rossa")'s funeral in [Dublin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dublin "Dublin").

## Year - 1914
 World War I: The [Swiss Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Military_of_Switzerland "Military of Switzerland") mobilizes because of World War I.

## Year - 1914
 [World War I](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_I "World War I"): The [German Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/German_Empire "German Empire") declares war on the [Russian Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Empire "Russian Empire").

## Year - 1911
 [Harriet Quimby](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Harriet_Quimby "Harriet Quimby") takes her pilot's test and becomes the first U.S. woman to earn an Aero Club of America aviator's certificate.

## Year - 1907
 The start of the [first Scout camp](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Brownsea_Island_Scout_camp "Brownsea Island Scout camp") on [Brownsea Island](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Brownsea_Island "Brownsea Island"), the origin of the worldwide [Scouting](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Scouting "Scouting") movement.

## Year - 1894
 The [Empire of Japan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Empire_of_Japan "Empire of Japan") and [Qing China](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Qing_dynasty "Qing dynasty") declare war on each other after a week of fighting over Korea, formally inaugurating the [First Sino-Japanese War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Sino-Japanese_War "First Sino-Japanese War").

## Year - 1893
 [Henry Perky](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_Perky "Henry Perky") patents [shredded wheat](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Shredded_wheat "Shredded wheat").

## Year - 1876
 [Colorado](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Colorado "Colorado") is admitted as the 38th [U.S. state](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/U.S._state "U.S. state").

## Year - 1863
 At the suggestion of Senator [J. V. Snellman](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Johan_Vilhelm_Snellman "Johan Vilhelm Snellman") and the order of Emperor [Alexander II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexander_II_of_Russia "Alexander II of Russia"), full rights are promised to the [Finnish language](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Finnish_language "Finnish language") by a language regulation in the [Grand Duchy of Finland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Finland "Grand Duchy of Finland").

## Year - 1855
 The first ascent of [Monte Rosa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Monte_Rosa "Monte Rosa"), the second highest summit in the Alps.

## Year - 1849
 <i>[Joven Daniel](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Joven_Daniel "Joven Daniel")</i> wrecks at the coast of [Araucanía](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Araucan%C3%ADa_(historic_region) "Araucanía (historic region)"), Chile, leading to allegations that local [Mapuche](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mapuche "Mapuche") tribes murdered survivors and kidnapped [Elisa Bravo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Elisa_Bravo "Elisa Bravo").

## Year - 1842
 The [Lombard Street riot](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lombard_Street_riot "Lombard Street riot") erupts in [Philadelphia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Philadelphia "Philadelphia"), [Pennsylvania](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pennsylvania "Pennsylvania"), United States.

## Year - 1834
 Construction begins on the [Wilberforce Monument](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilberforce_Monument "Wilberforce Monument") in [Kingston Upon Hull](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingston_Upon_Hull "Kingston Upon Hull").

## Year - 1834
 [Slavery is abolished in the British Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Abolitionism_in_the_United_Kingdom "Abolitionism in the United Kingdom") as the [Slavery Abolition Act 1833](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Slavery_Abolition_Act_1833 "Slavery Abolition Act 1833") comes into force, although it remains legal in the possessions of the [East India Company](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/East_India_Company "East India Company") until the passage of the [Indian Slavery Act, 1843](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_Slavery_Act,_1843 "Indian Slavery Act, 1843").

## Year - 1801
 [First Barbary War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Barbary_War "First Barbary War"): The American [schooner](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Schooner "Schooner") [USS <i>Enterprise</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/USS_Enterprise_(1799) "USS Enterprise (1799)") [captures](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Action_of_1_August_1801 "Action of 1 August 1801") the [Tripolitan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tripoli,_Libya "Tripoli, Libya") [polacca](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Polacca "Polacca") <i>Tripoli</i> in a [single-ship action](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Single-ship_action "Single-ship action") off the coast of modern-day [Libya](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Libya "Libya").

## Year - 1800
 The [Acts of Union 1800](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Acts_of_Union_1800 "Acts of Union 1800") are passed which merge the [Kingdom of Great Britain](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Great_Britain "Kingdom of Great Britain") and the [Kingdom of Ireland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Ireland "Kingdom of Ireland") into the [United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom_of_Great_Britain_and_Ireland "United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland").

## Year - 1798
 [French Revolutionary Wars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Revolutionary_Wars "French Revolutionary Wars"): [Battle of the Nile (Battle of Aboukir Bay)](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_the_Nile "Battle of the Nile"): Battle begins when a British fleet engages the French Revolutionary Navy fleet in an unusual night action.

## Year - 1774
 British scientist [Joseph Priestley](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Joseph_Priestley "Joseph Priestley") discovers [oxygen](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Oxygen "Oxygen") gas, corroborating the prior discovery of this element by German-Swedish chemist [Carl Wilhelm Scheele](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Carl_Wilhelm_Scheele "Carl Wilhelm Scheele").

## Year - 1759
 [Seven Years' War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Seven_Years%27_War "Seven Years' War"): The [Battle of Minden](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Minden "Battle of Minden"), an allied Anglo-German army victory over the French. In Britain this was one of a number of events that constituted the [Annus Mirabilis of 1759](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Annus_Mirabilis_of_1759 "Annus Mirabilis of 1759") and is celebrated as [Minden Day](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Minden_Day "Minden Day") by certain British Army regiments.

## Year - 1714
 George, Elector of [Hanover](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hanover "Hanover"), becomes King [George I of Great Britain](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/George_I_of_Great_Britain "George I of Great Britain"), marking the beginning of the [Georgian era](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Georgian_era "Georgian era") of British history.

## Year - 1664
 [Ottoman forces](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ottoman_Empire "Ottoman Empire") are defeated in the [battle of Saint Gotthard](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Saint_Gotthard_(1664) "Battle of Saint Gotthard (1664)") by an [Austrian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Habsburg_monarchy "Habsburg monarchy") army led by [Raimondo Montecuccoli](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Raimondo_Montecuccoli "Raimondo Montecuccoli"), resulting in the [Peace of Vasvár](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Peace_of_Vasv%C3%A1r "Peace of Vasvár").

## Year - 1620
 [<i>Speedwell</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Speedwell_(1577_ship) "Speedwell (1577 ship)") leaves [Delfshaven](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Delfshaven "Delfshaven") to bring [pilgrims](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pilgrim_Fathers "Pilgrim Fathers") to [America](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Plymouth_Colony "Plymouth Colony") by way of England.

## Year - 1571
 The [Ottoman conquest of Cyprus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ottoman%E2%80%93Venetian_War_(1570%E2%80%9373)#Ottoman_conquest_of_Cyprus "Ottoman-Venetian War (1570-73)") is concluded, by the surrender of [Famagusta](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Famagusta "Famagusta").

## Year - 1498
 [Christopher Columbus](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Christopher_Columbus "Christopher Columbus") becomes the first European to visit what is now [Venezuela](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Venezuela "Venezuela").

## Year - 1469
 [Louis XI of France](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_XI_of_France "Louis XI of France") founds the [chivalric order](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chivalric_order "Chivalric order") called the [Order of Saint Michael](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Order_of_Saint_Michael "Order of Saint Michael") in [Amboise](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Amboise "Amboise").

## Year - 1291
 The [Old Swiss Confederacy](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_Swiss_Confederacy "Old Swiss Confederacy") is formed with the signature of the [Federal Charter](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Charter_of_1291 "Federal Charter of 1291").

## Year - 1203
 [Isaac II Angelos](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Isaac_II_Angelos "Isaac II Angelos"), restored [Byzantine Emperor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Emperor "Byzantine Emperor"), declares his son [Alexios IV Angelos](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexios_IV_Angelos "Alexios IV Angelos") co-emperor after pressure from the forces of the [Fourth Crusade](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Fourth_Crusade "Fourth Crusade").

## Year - 902
[Taormina](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Taormina "Taormina"), the last Byzantine stronghold in [Sicily](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sicily "Sicily"), is [captured](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Siege_of_Taormina_(902) "Siege of Taormina (902)") by the [Aghlabid](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Aghlabid "Aghlabid") army, concluding the [Muslim conquest of Sicily](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Muslim_conquest_of_Sicily "Muslim conquest of Sicily").

## Year - 607
[Ono no Imoko](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ono_no_Imoko "Ono no Imoko") is dispatched as envoy to the [Sui](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sui_dynasty "Sui dynasty") court in China (Traditional [Japanese date](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_calendar "Japanese calendar"): July 3, 607).

## Year - 527
[Justinian I](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Justinian_I "Justinian I") becomes the sole ruler of the [Byzantine Empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Empire "Byzantine Empire").

## Year - 69
D 69 – [Batavian rebellion](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Revolt_of_the_Batavi "Revolt of the Batavi"): The [Batavians](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Batavi_(Germanic_tribe) "Batavi (Germanic tribe)") in [Germania Inferior](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Germania_Inferior "Germania Inferior") (Netherlands) revolt under the leadership of [Gaius Julius Civilis](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaius_Julius_Civilis "Gaius Julius Civilis").

## Year - 30 BC
- 30 BC – [Octavian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Augustus "Augustus") (later known as Augustus) enters [Alexandria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexandria "Alexandria"), [Egypt](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Egypt "Egypt"), bringing it under the control of the [Roman Republic](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_Republic "Roman Republic").
