
# Today's History 📜

16th April 2024


## Year - 2018
 <i>[The New York Times](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_New_York_Times "The New York Times")</i> and the <i>[New Yorker](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_New_Yorker "The New Yorker")</i> win the [Pulitzer Prize for Public Service](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pulitzer_Prize_for_Public_Service "Pulitzer Prize for Public Service") for breaking news of the [Harvey Weinstein sexual abuse scandal](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Harvey_Weinstein_sexual_abuse_cases "Harvey Weinstein sexual abuse cases").

## Year - 2016
 [Ecuador's worst earthquake in nearly 40 years](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2016_Ecuador_earthquake "2016 Ecuador earthquake") kills 676 and injures 6,274.

## Year - 2014
 The [South Korean](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Korea "South Korea") ferry [MV <i>Sewol</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/MV_Sewol "MV Sewol") [capsizes and sinks](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sinking_of_MV_Sewol "Sinking of MV Sewol") near [Jindo Island](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jindo_(island) "Jindo (island)"), killing 304 passengers and crew and leading to widespread criticism of the South Korean government, media, and shipping authorities.

## Year - 2013
 The [2013 Baga massacre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2013_Baga_massacre "2013 Baga massacre") is started when [Boko Haram](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Boko_Haram "Boko Haram") militants engage government soldiers in [Baga](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Baga,_Borno "Baga, Borno").

## Year - 2013
 A 7.8-[magnitude](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Moment_magnitude_scale "Moment magnitude scale") [earthquake](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2013_Saravan_earthquake "2013 Saravan earthquake") strikes [Sistan and Baluchestan Province](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sistan_and_Baluchestan_Province "Sistan and Baluchestan Province"), [Iran](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran"), killing at least 35 people and injuring 117 others.

## Year - 2012
 The [Pulitzer Prize](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2012_Pulitzer_Prize "2012 Pulitzer Prize") winners were announced, it was the first time [since 1977](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1977_Pulitzer_Prize "1977 Pulitzer Prize") that no book won the [Fiction Prize](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pulitzer_Prize_for_Fiction "Pulitzer Prize for Fiction").

## Year - 2012
 The [trial](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Trial_of_Anders_Behring_Breivik "Trial of Anders Behring Breivik") for [Anders Behring Breivik](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Anders_Behring_Breivik "Anders Behring Breivik"), the perpetrator of the [2011 Norway attacks](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2011_Norway_attacks "2011 Norway attacks"), begins in [Oslo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Oslo "Oslo"), Norway.

## Year - 2008
 The [U.S. Supreme Court](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_the_United_States "Supreme Court of the United States") rules in the <i>[Baze v. Rees](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Baze_v._Rees "Baze v. Rees")</i> decision that [execution by lethal injection](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lethal_injection "Lethal injection") does not violate the [Eighth Amendment](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Eighth_Amendment_to_the_United_States_Constitution "Eighth Amendment to the United States Constitution") ban against [cruel and unusual punishment](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cruel_and_unusual_punishment "Cruel and unusual punishment").

## Year - 2007
 [Virginia Tech shooting](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Virginia_Tech_shooting "Virginia Tech shooting"): [Seung-Hui Cho](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Seung-Hui_Cho "Seung-Hui Cho") guns down 32 people and injures 17 before committing suicide.

## Year - 2003
 The [Treaty of Accession](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Accession_2003 "Treaty of Accession 2003") is signed in [Athens](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Athens "Athens") admitting ten new member states to the [European Union](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Union "European Union").

## Year - 2001
 India and [Bangladesh](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bangladesh "Bangladesh") begin a [five-day border conflict](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/2001_Bangladeshi-Indian_border_skirmish "2001 Bangladeshi-Indian border skirmish"), but are unable to resolve the disputes about [their border](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/India%E2%80%93Bangladesh_border "India-Bangladesh border").

## Year - 1972
 [Apollo program](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo_program "Apollo program"): The launch of [Apollo 16](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo_16 "Apollo 16") from [Cape Canaveral, Florida](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cape_Canaveral,_Florida "Cape Canaveral, Florida").

## Year - 1963
 U.S. civil rights campaigner Dr. [Martin Luther King Jr.](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Martin_Luther_King_Jr. "Martin Luther King Jr.") writes his open [letter from Birmingham Jail](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Letter_from_Birmingham_Jail "Letter from Birmingham Jail"), sometimes known as "The Negro Is Your Brother", while incarcerated in [Birmingham, Alabama](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Birmingham,_Alabama "Birmingham, Alabama"), for protesting against segregation.

## Year - 1961
 In a nationally broadcast speech, Cuban leader [Fidel Castro](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Fidel_Castro "Fidel Castro") declares that he is a [Marxist-Leninist](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Marxism%E2%80%93Leninism "Marxism-Leninism") and that Cuba is going to adopt [Communism](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Communism "Communism").

## Year - 1948
 The [Organization of European Economic Co-operation](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Organization_of_European_Economic_Co-operation "Organization of European Economic Co-operation") is formed.

## Year - 1947
 [Bernard Baruch](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bernard_Baruch "Bernard Baruch") first applies the term "[Cold War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cold_War "Cold War")" to describe the relationship between the United States and the [Soviet Union](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union").

## Year - 1947
 An [explosion](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas_City_disaster "Texas City disaster") on board a freighter in port causes the city of [Texas City, Texas](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas_City,_Texas "Texas City, Texas"), United States, to catch fire, killing almost 600 people.

## Year - 1945
 More than 7,000 die when the German transport ship <i>[Goya](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Goya_(ship) "Goya (ship)")</i> is sunk by a Soviet [submarine](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Submarine "Submarine").

## Year - 1945
 The [United States Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Army "United States Army") liberates Nazi <i>Sonderlager</i> (high security) [prisoner-of-war camp](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prisoner-of-war_camp "Prisoner-of-war camp") [Oflag IV-C](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Oflag_IV-C "Oflag IV-C") (better known as [Colditz](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Colditz_Castle "Colditz Castle")).

## Year - 1945
 World War II: The [Red Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Army "Red Army") begins the [final assault](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Berlin "Battle of Berlin") on [German](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazi_Germany "Nazi Germany") forces around Berlin, with nearly one million troops fighting in the [Battle of the Seelow Heights](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_the_Seelow_Heights "Battle of the Seelow Heights").

## Year - 1944
 World War II: Allied forces start [bombing Belgrade](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Allied_bombing_of_Yugoslavia_in_World_War_II "Allied bombing of Yugoslavia in World War II"), killing about 1,100 people. This bombing fell on the Orthodox Christian Easter.

## Year - 1943
 [Albert Hofmann](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Albert_Hofmann "Albert Hofmann") accidentally discovers the hallucinogenic effects of the research drug [LSD](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/LSD "LSD"). He intentionally takes the drug three days later on [April 19](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/April_19 "April 19").

## Year - 1942
 King [George VI](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/George_VI "George VI") awarded the [George Cross to the people of Malta](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Award_of_the_George_Cross_to_Malta "Award of the George Cross to Malta") in appreciation of their heroism.

## Year - 1941
 World War II: The Nazi-affiliated [Ustaše](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Usta%C5%A1e "Usta") is put in charge of the [Independent State of Croatia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Independent_State_of_Croatia "Independent State of Croatia") by the [Axis powers](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Axis_powers "Axis powers") after [Operation 25](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Invasion_of_Yugoslavia "Invasion of Yugoslavia") is effected.

## Year - 1941
 [World War II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II "World War II"): The Italian-German <i>Tarigo</i> convoy is [attacked and destroyed](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_the_Tarigo_Convoy "Battle of the Tarigo Convoy") by British ships.

## Year - 1925
 During the [Communist](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Communist "Communist") [St Nedelya Church assault](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/St_Nedelya_Church_assault "St Nedelya Church assault") in [Sofia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sofia "Sofia"), [Bulgaria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Bulgaria "Kingdom of Bulgaria"), 150 are killed and 500 are wounded.

## Year - 1922
 The [Treaty of Rapallo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_Rapallo_(1922) "Treaty of Rapallo (1922)"), pursuant to which [Germany](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Weimar_Republic "Weimar Republic") and the [Soviet Union](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union") re-establish [diplomatic relations](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Diplomatic_relations "Diplomatic relations"), is signed.

## Year - 1919
 [Polish-Lithuanian War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Polish%E2%80%93Lithuanian_War "Polish-Lithuanian War"): The [Polish Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Polish_Land_Forces "Polish Land Forces") launches the [Vilna offensive](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vilna_offensive "Vilna offensive") to capture [Vilnius](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vilnius "Vilnius") in modern [Lithuania](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lithuania "Lithuania").

## Year - 1919
 [Mohandas Gandhi](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahatma_Gandhi "Mahatma Gandhi") organizes a day of "prayer and fasting" in response to the killing of Indian protesters in the [Jallianwala Bagh massacre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jallianwala_Bagh_massacre "Jallianwala Bagh massacre") by the British colonial troops three days earlier.

## Year - 1917
 [Russian Revolution](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Revolution "Russian Revolution"): [Vladimir Lenin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vladimir_Lenin "Vladimir Lenin") returns to [Petrograd](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Petersburg "Saint Petersburg"), [Russia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_Provisional_Government "Russian Provisional Government"), from exile in Switzerland.

## Year - 1912
 [Harriet Quimby](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Harriet_Quimby "Harriet Quimby") becomes the first woman to fly an [airplane](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Aircraft "Aircraft") across the [English Channel](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/English_Channel "English Channel").

## Year - 1910
 The oldest existing indoor [ice hockey](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ice_hockey "Ice hockey") arena still used for the sport in the 21st century, [Boston Arena](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Matthews_Arena "Matthews Arena"), opens for the first time.

## Year - 1908
 [Natural Bridges National Monument](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Natural_Bridges_National_Monument "Natural Bridges National Monument") is established in [Utah](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Utah "Utah").

## Year - 1881
 In [Dodge City, Kansas](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dodge_City,_Kansas "Dodge City, Kansas"), [Bat Masterson](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bat_Masterson "Bat Masterson") fights his last gun battle.

## Year - 1878
 The [Senate of the Grand Duchy of Finland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Senate_of_the_Grand_Duchy_of_Finland "Senate of the Grand Duchy of Finland") issued a declaration establishing a city of [Kotka](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kotka "Kotka") on the southern part islands from the [old Kymi parish](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kymi,_Finland "Kymi, Finland").

## Year - 1863
 American Civil War: During the [Vicksburg Campaign](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vicksburg_Campaign "Vicksburg Campaign"), [gunboats](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Gunboat "Gunboat") commanded by acting Rear Admiral [David Dixon Porter](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Dixon_Porter "David Dixon Porter") run downriver past Confederate [artillery batteries](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Artillery_battery "Artillery battery") at Vicksburg.

## Year - 1862
 American Civil War: The [District of Columbia Compensated Emancipation Act](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/District_of_Columbia_Compensated_Emancipation_Act "District of Columbia Compensated Emancipation Act"), a bill ending [slavery in the District of Columbia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Slavery_in_the_District_of_Columbia "Slavery in the District of Columbia"), becomes law.

## Year - 1862
 [American Civil War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Civil_War "American Civil War"): [Battle at Lee's Mills](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_at_Lee%27s_Mills "Battle at Lee's Mills") in [Virginia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Virginia "Virginia").

## Year - 1858
 The [Wernerian Natural History Society](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wernerian_Natural_History_Society "Wernerian Natural History Society"), a former Scottish [learned society](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Learned_society "Learned society"), is dissolved.

## Year - 1853
 The [Great Indian Peninsula Railway](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Indian_Peninsula_Railway "Great Indian Peninsula Railway") opens the first passenger rail in India, from [Bori Bunder](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bori_Bunder "Bori Bunder") to [Thane](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Thane "Thane").

## Year - 1847
 Shooting of a [Māori](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/M%C4%81ori_people "M") by an English sailor results in the opening of the [Wanganui Campaign](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wanganui_Campaign "Wanganui Campaign") of the [New Zealand Wars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand_Wars "New Zealand Wars").

## Year - 1838
 The [French Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Army "French Army") [captures Veracruz](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Veracruz_(1838) "Battle of Veracruz (1838)") in the [Pastry War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Pastry_War "Pastry War").

## Year - 1818
 The [United States Senate](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Senate "United States Senate") ratifies the [Rush-Bagot Treaty](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Rush%E2%80%93Bagot_Treaty "Rush-Bagot Treaty"), limiting naval armaments on the [Great Lakes](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Lakes "Great Lakes") and [Lake Champlain](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Champlain "Lake Champlain").

## Year - 1799
 [French Revolutionary Wars](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/French_Revolutionary_Wars "French Revolutionary Wars"): The [Battle of Mount Tabor](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Mount_Tabor_(1799) "Battle of Mount Tabor (1799)"): [Napoleon](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Napoleon "Napoleon") drives [Ottoman Turks](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ottoman_Turks "Ottoman Turks") across the [River Jordan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/River_Jordan "River Jordan") near [Acre](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Acre,_Israel "Acre, Israel").

## Year - 1780
 [Franz Friedrich Wilhelm von Fürstenberg](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Franz_Friedrich_Wilhelm_von_F%C3%BCrstenberg "Franz Friedrich Wilhelm von F") founds the [University of Münster](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_M%C3%BCnster "University of M").

## Year - 1746
 The [Battle of Culloden](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Culloden "Battle of Culloden") is fought between the French-supported [Jacobites](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacobitism "Jacobitism") and the British [Hanoverian](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/House_of_Hanover "House of Hanover") forces commanded by [William Augustus, Duke of Cumberland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_William,_Duke_of_Cumberland "Prince William, Duke of Cumberland"), in Scotland. After the battle many highland traditions were banned and the [Highlands of Scotland were cleared of inhabitants](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Highland_Clearances "Highland Clearances").

## Year - 1582
 Spanish [conquistador](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Conquistador "Conquistador") [Hernando de Lerma](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hernando_de_Lerma "Hernando de Lerma") founds the settlement of [Salta](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Salta "Salta"), Argentina.

## Year - 1520
 The [Revolt of the Comuneros](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Revolt_of_the_Comuneros "Revolt of the Comuneros") begins in Spain against the rule of [Charles V](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_V,_Holy_Roman_Emperor "Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor").

## Year - 1346
 [Stefan Dušan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Stefan_Du%C5%A1an "Stefan Du"), "the Mighty", is crowned [Emperor of the Serbs](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor_of_the_Serbs "Emperor of the Serbs") at [Skopje](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Skopje "Skopje"), [his empire](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Serbian_Empire "Serbian Empire") occupying much of the [Balkans](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Balkans "Balkans").

## Year - 73
Masada](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Masada "Masada"), a Jewish fortress, falls to the [Romans](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_Empire "Roman Empire") after several months of siege, ending the [First Jewish-Roman War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Jewish%E2%80%93Roman_War "First Jewish-Roman War").

## Year - 69
efeated by [Vitellius](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Vitellius "Vitellius")' troops at Bedriacum, Roman emperor [Otho](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Otho "Otho") commits suicide.

## Year - 1457 BC
C - 1457 BC – [Battle of Megido](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Megiddo_(15th_century_BC) "Battle of Megiddo (15th century BC)") - the first [battle](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle "Battle") to have been recorded in what is accepted as relatively reliable detail.
