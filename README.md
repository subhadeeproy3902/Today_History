## Year - 1967
 The [Northrop M2-F2](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Northrop_M2-F2 "Northrop M2-F2") crashes on landing, becoming the inspiration for the novel <i>[Cyborg](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyborg_(novel) "Cyborg (novel)")</i> and TV series <i>[The Six Million Dollar Man](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Six_Million_Dollar_Man "The Six Million Dollar Man")</i>.

 [Air France Flight 406](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Air_France_Flight_406 "Air France Flight 406") is destroyed by a bomb over the [Sahara](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sahara "Sahara"), killing 78.

## Year - 1946
 First successful launch of an American [V-2 rocket](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/V-2_rocket "V-2 rocket") at [White Sands Proving Ground](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/White_Sands_Missile_Range "White Sands Missile Range").

## Year - 1942
 World War II: The Thai [Phayap Army](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Phayap_Army "Phayap Army") invades the [Shan States](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Shan_States "Shan States") during the [Burma Campaign](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Burma_Campaign "Burma Campaign").

## Year - 1941
 World War II: [Rudolf Hess](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Rudolf_Hess "Rudolf Hess") parachutes into Scotland to try to negotiate a peace deal between the United Kingdom and [Nazi Germany](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazi_Germany "Nazi Germany").

## Year - 1941
 World War II: The [House of Commons](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/British_House_of_Commons "British House of Commons") in London is damaged by the [Luftwaffe](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Luftwaffe "Luftwaffe") in an [air raid](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Airstrike "Airstrike").

## Year - 1940
 World War II: [Winston Churchill](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Winston_Churchill "Winston Churchill") is appointed [Prime Minister of the United Kingdom](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_the_United_Kingdom "Prime Minister of the United Kingdom") following the resignation of [Neville Chamberlain](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Neville_Chamberlain "Neville Chamberlain"). On the same day, Germany invades France, The Netherlands, Belgium and Luxembourg. Meanwhile, the United Kingdom [occupies Iceland](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Invasion_of_Iceland "Invasion of Iceland").

## Year - 1940
 [World War II](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/World_War_II "World War II"): German fighters accidentally bomb the German city of [Freiburg](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Freiburg "Freiburg").

## Year - 1933
 [Censorship](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Censorship "Censorship"): In Germany, the [Nazis](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazism "Nazism") stage [massive public book burnings](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazi_book_burnings "Nazi book burnings").

Edgar Hoover") is appointed first Director of the United States' [Federal Bureau of Investigation](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Bureau_of_Investigation "Federal Bureau of Investigation") (FBI), and remains so until his death in [1972](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1972 "1972").

## Year - 1922
 The United States annexes the [Kingman Reef](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingman_Reef "Kingman Reef").

## Year - 1916
 Sailing in the lifeboat [<i>James Caird</i>](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Voyage_of_the_James_Caird "Voyage of the James Caird"), [Ernest Shackleton](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernest_Shackleton "Ernest Shackleton") arrives at [South Georgia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Georgia_and_the_South_Sandwich_Islands "South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands") after a journey of 800 nautical miles from [Elephant Island](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Elephant_Island "Elephant Island").

## Year - 1908
 [Mother's Day](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mother%27s_Day "Mother's Day") is observed for the first time in the United States, in [Grafton, West Virginia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grafton,_West_Virginia "Grafton, West Virginia").

## Year - 1904
 The [Horch &amp; Cir. Motorwagenwerke AG](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Horch "Horch") is founded. It would eventually become the [Audi](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Audi "Audi") company.

## Year - 1899
 Finnish farmworker [Karl Emil Malmelin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Karl_Emil_Malmelin "Karl Emil Malmelin") kills seven people with an axe at the Simola croft in the village of [Klaukkala](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Klaukkala "Klaukkala").

## Year - 1881
 [Carol I](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Carol_I_of_Romania "Carol I of Romania") is crowned the [King](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/King_of_Romania "King of Romania") of the [Romanian Kingdom](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Romania "Kingdom of Romania").

## Year - 1876

## Year - 1872
 [Victoria Woodhull](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Victoria_Woodhull "Victoria Woodhull") becomes the first woman nominated for [President of the United States](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_the_United_States "President of the United States").

## Year - 1869
 The [First transcontinental railroad](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/First_transcontinental_railroad "First transcontinental railroad"), linking the eastern and western United States, is completed at [Promontory Summit](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Promontory_Summit "Promontory Summit"), [Utah Territory](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Utah_Territory "Utah Territory") with the [golden spike](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Golden_spike "Golden spike").

## Year - 1865

[Sepoys](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Sepoy "Sepoy") mutiny against their commanding officers at [Meerut](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Meerut "Meerut").

## Year - 1837
 [Panic of 1837](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Panic_of_1837 "Panic of 1837"): New York City banks suspend the payment of [specie](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Coin "Coin"), triggering a national banking crisis and an [economic depression](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Depression_(economics) "Depression (economics)") whose severity was not surpassed until the [Great Depression](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Depression_in_the_United_States "Great Depression in the United States").

## Year - 1833

The Austrians lose some 2,000 men.

## Year - 1775

## Year - 1775

## Year - 1774

## Year - 1773
 The [Parliament of Great Britain](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Parliament_of_Great_Britain "Parliament of Great Britain") passes the [Tea Act](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tea_Act "Tea Act"), designed to save the [British East India Company](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/British_East_India_Company "British East India Company") by reducing taxes on its [tea](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tea "Tea") and granting it the right to sell tea directly to North America. ## Year - 1768

## Year - 1688
