## Year - 2020

## Year - 2007
 At [AT&amp;T Park](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/AT%26T_Park "AT&amp;T Park"), [Barry Bonds](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Barry_Bonds "Barry Bonds") hits his 756th career [home run](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Home_run "Home run") to surpass [Hank Aaron](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hank_Aaron "Hank Aaron")'s 33-year-old record.

## Year - 1999
 The [Chechnya](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chechnya "Chechnya")-based [Islamic International Brigade](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_International_Brigade "Islamic International Brigade") [invades](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_Dagestan "War of Dagestan") neighboring [Dagestan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dagestan "Dagestan").

## Year - 1998
 [Bombings at United States embassies](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1998_United_States_embassy_bombings "1998 United States embassy bombings") in [Dar es Salaam, Tanzania](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Dar_es_Salaam "Dar es Salaam") and [Nairobi, Kenya](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Nairobi "Nairobi") kill approximately 212 people.

## Year - 1997
 [Fine Air Flight 101](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Fine_Air_Flight_101 "Fine Air Flight 101") crashes after takeoff from [Miami International Airport](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Miami_International_Airport "Miami International Airport"), killing five people.

## Year - 1995
 The Chilean government declares [state of emergency](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/State_of_emergency "State of emergency") in the southern half of the country in response to an event of intense, cold, wind, rain and snowfall known as the [White Earthquake](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/White_Earthquake "White Earthquake").

## Year - 1993
 [Ada Deer](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ada_Deer "Ada Deer"), a [Menominee](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Menominee "Menominee") activist, is sworn in as the head of the [Bureau of Indian Affairs](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bureau_of_Indian_Affairs "Bureau of Indian Affairs").

## Year - 1990

## Year - 1989

## Year - 1987

## Year - 1985
 [Takao Doi](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Takao_Doi "Takao Doi"), [Mamoru Mohri](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Mamoru_Mohri "Mamoru Mohri") and [Chiaki Mukai](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Chiaki_Mukai "Chiaki Mukai") are chosen to be Japan's first [astronauts](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Astronaut "Astronaut").

## Year - 1981

## Year - 1976
