## Year - 2020

## Year - 2013
 More than 100 people are killed when anti-government forces [attack](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/December_2013_Kinshasa_attacks "December 2013 Kinshasa attacks") key buildings in [Kinshasa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kinshasa "Kinshasa"), [Democratic Republic of the Congo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo "Democratic Republic of the Congo").

## Year - 2009

## Year - 2009
 A segment of the [Lanzhou-Zhengzhou-Changsha pipeline](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lanzhou%E2%80%93Zhengzhou%E2%80%93Changsha_pipeline "Lanzhou-Zhengzhou-Changsha pipeline") ruptures in [Shaanxi](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Shaanxi "Shaanxi"), China, and approximately 150,000ﾂL (40,000ﾂUSﾂgal) of [diesel oil](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Diesel_fuel "Diesel fuel") [flows down](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Yellow_River_oil_spill "Yellow River oil spill") the [Wei River](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wei_River "Wei River") before finally reaching the [Yellow River](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Yellow_River "Yellow River").

## Year - 2006

## Year - 2006

## Year - 2006

## Year - 2005
 [Tropical Storm Zeta](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tropical_Storm_Zeta_(2005) "Tropical Storm Zeta (2005)") forms in the open Atlantic Ocean, tying the record for the latest [tropical cyclone](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tropical_cyclone "Tropical cyclone") ever to form in the North Atlantic basin.

## Year - 2000

## Year - 1997
 In the worst incident in [Algeria](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Algeria "Algeria")'s insurgency, the [Wilaya of Relizane massacres](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilaya_of_Relizane_massacres_of_30_December_1997 "Wilaya of Relizane massacres of 30 December 1997"), 400 people from four villages are killed.

## Year - 1996
 Proposed budget cuts by [Benjamin Netanyahu](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Benjamin_Netanyahu "Benjamin Netanyahu") spark protests from 250,000 workers who shut down services across Israel.

## Year - 1993

## Year - 1987
 [Stella Sigcau](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Stella_Sigcau "Stella Sigcau"), Prime minister of the South African [Bantustan](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bantustan "Bantustan") of Transkei, is ousted from power in a [bloodless military coup](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1987_Transkei_coup_d%27%C3%A9tat "1987 Transkei coup d'ﾃｩtat") led by General [Bantu Holomisa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Bantu_Holomisa "Bantu Holomisa").

## Year - 1970
 [Hurricane Creek mine disaster](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hurricane_Creek_mine_disaster "Hurricane Creek mine disaster"), near [Hyden, Kentucky](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyden,_Kentucky "Hyden, Kentucky")

## Year - 1954
 The [Finnish National Bureau of Investigation](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Bureau_of_Investigation_(Finland) "National Bureau of Investigation (Finland)") is established to consolidate [criminal investigation](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Criminal_investigation "Criminal investigation") and [intelligence](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Criminal_intelligence "Criminal intelligence") into a single agency.

## Year - 1952
 An RAF [Avro Lancaster](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Avro_Lancaster "Avro Lancaster") bomber [crashes](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/1952_Luqa_Avro_Lancaster_crash "1952 Luqa Avro Lancaster crash") in [Luqa](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Luqa "Luqa"), Malta after an engine failure, killing three crew members and a civilian on the ground.

## Year - 1947

## Year - 1944

## Year - 1943

## Year - 1936
 The [Flint sit-down strike](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Flint_sit-down_strike "Flint sit-down strike") hits General Motors.

## Year - 1935

## Year - 1927
 The [Ginza Line](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Tokyo_Metro_Ginza_Line "Tokyo Metro Ginza Line"), the first [subway line](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Rapid_transit "Rapid transit") in Asia, opens in Tokyo, Japan.

## Year - 1922
 The [Union of Soviet Socialist Republics](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union") (USSR) [is formed](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_on_the_Creation_of_the_USSR "Treaty on the Creation of the USSR").

## Year - 1916
 The last [coronation in Hungary](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Coronation_of_the_Hungarian_monarch "Coronation of the Hungarian monarch") is performed for King [Charles IV](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_I_of_Austria "Charles I of Austria") and Queen [Zita](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Zita_of_Bourbon-Parma "Zita of Bourbon-Parma").

## Year - 1916
 Russian mystic and advisor to the Tsar [Grigori Yefimovich Rasputin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Grigori_Yefimovich_Rasputin "Grigori Yefimovich Rasputin") is murdered by a loyalist group led by Prince [Felix Yusupov](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Felix_Yusupov "Felix Yusupov"). His frozen, partially-trussed body was discovered in a Petrograd river three days later.

## Year - 1906

## Year - 1905

## Year - 1903

## Year - 1902

## Year - 1896
 Canadian [ice hockey](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ice_hockey "Ice hockey") player [Ernie McLea](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernie_McLea "Ernie McLea") scores the first [hat-trick](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Hat-trick "Hat-trick") in [Stanley Cup](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Stanley_Cup "Stanley Cup") play, and the Cup-winning goal as the [Montreal Victorias](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Montreal_Victorias "Montreal Victorias") defeat the [Winnipeg Victorias](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Winnipeg_Victorias "Winnipeg Victorias") 6-5.

## Year - 1896

## Year - 1853

## Year - 1825
 The [Treaty of St. Louis](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_St._Louis_(1825) "Treaty of St. Louis (1825)") between the United States and the [Shawnee](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Shawnee "Shawnee") Nation is proclaimed.

## Year - 1816

## Year - 1813
 [War of 1812](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_1812 "War of 1812"): British soldiers [burn Buffalo](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Buffalo "Battle of Buffalo"), New York.

## Year - 1702

## Year - 1641
 [Reapers' War](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Reapers%27_War "Reapers' War"): [Louis XIII of France](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_XIII "Louis XIII") swears the [Catalan constitutions](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Catalan_constitutions "Catalan constitutions"), being appointed [Count of Barcelona](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Count_of_Barcelona "Count of Barcelona") and thus establishing the [personal union](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Personal_union "Personal union") of the [Principality of Catalonia](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Principality_of_Catalonia "Principality of Catalonia") with the [Kingdom of France](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_France "Kingdom of France").

## Year - 1460
 [Wars of the Roses](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Wars_of_the_Roses "Wars of the Roses"): Lancastrians kill the [3rd Duke of York](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_of_York,_3rd_Duke_of_York "Richard of York, 3rd Duke of York") and win the [Battle of Wakefield](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Wakefield "Battle of Wakefield").

## Year - 1419

## Year - 999
[Battle of Glenmama](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Glenmama "Battle of Glenmama"): The combined forces of [Munster](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Munster "Munster") and [Meath](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Meath "Kingdom of Meath") under king [Brian Boru](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Brian_Boru "Brian Boru") inflict a crushing defeat on the allied armies of [Leinster](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Leinster "Leinster") and [Dublin](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Dublin "Kingdom of Dublin") near [Lyons Hill](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Lyons_Hill "Lyons Hill") in Ireland.

## Year - 534
